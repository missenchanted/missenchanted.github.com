We have Monday blues and why not Monday blacks, since for me blue is a very soothing and calm color, although its very difficult to find the right blue pigmentation. I received this pencil in the February edition of Blisscovered, which is back on the path of self discovery I suppose.

Price: Rs. 370,  but online it can be found around Rs. 290

Claim: Chambor stay-on eye pencils provide rich,long, long lasting and consistent color.We love the pencil's creamy texture and the rich, deep pigmentation of the midnight blue.its almost black, so can be worn day & night.Its great for an edgy winged liner look.

  • Twist up – One Way,
  • Water Proof – Stay on and Smudgeproof,
  • Vitamin Enriched: Vitamin – E & A, No Animal Ingredients,
  • Non – Comodogenic, Dermatologist Tested,
  • Opthalmologist Tested
  My Experience: This pencil lives up to all expectations, and delivers a fabulous pigmentation. Completely waterproof and doesn't smudge at all. What I did not like, its one way twist up, one its out there is no way to go back and the product gets  wasted.   What I like:
  1. Nice pigmentation,
  2. Good lasting power, 6-7 hours without fading,
  3. Water and smudge proof,
What I did not like:
  1. One way twist up, why it has to be this way
My best friend recommended me the brown from Chambor range and I am definitely picking up another color. Have you tried Chambor pencils?
