I am so excited to introduce to you all, one of the my favorite ladies Mrs. Valerie Madre Deus, who inspires me with her innovative thoughts and endeavors to make this earth a more cleaner and beautiful place to live in. Her thoughts led me to waste segregation, in my house you will find three kind of waste bins,

  1. Organic - Vegetables peels etc,
  2. Recyclable - Paper, cardboard, recyclable plastic - handover to some organization once every week
  3. Dry/ E -waste - Harmful chemicals, plastic, batteries etc
