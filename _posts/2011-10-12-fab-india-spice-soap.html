This time its their Simulating spice soap. Claims : Stimulating and clarifying bathing bar with a combination of invigorating ginger oil, rosemary oil, nutmeg oil, cinnamon oil , crushed cloves and crushed cardamom seeds. Price: Rs.65 Its same for all bars :) Contains: Sodium Laureth Sulphate, Cocobetaine, Saponified Castor Oil, Cotton Seed Oil, Coconut Oil, Glycerine, Propylene Glycol, Sodium Stearate, Sodium Laurate, Ginger Oil, Rosemary Oil, Nutmeg Oil, Cinnamon Oil, Crushed Cloves, Crushed Cardamom Seeds. Pros:
  1. Pricing is very decent, apart from that nothing exceptional about it.
Cons:
  1. Nothing like simulating as Lavender & Basil soap :(
  2. One side was like a spice scrub, but after 2-3 washes no scrub was left... Fabindia should seriously mix it a bit better
I wont buy this again, as there is nothing exceptional about it. Have you tried any spice soap or Scrub ?
