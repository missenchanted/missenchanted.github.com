That's the only slot of time I find myself for blogging and interacting with people which I just cannot miss by any chance. I happen to read about Naturalmantra.com on Siri's blog, and within 15 min of reading I had placed my first order.This website has to be a one stop destination for all natural and Organic products in India.

My Experience : I placed my order on 5th Feb and received it on 8th.

I made use of their Valentine offer and got a discount of Rs.250...:) and picked up a combo of Soulflower soap. I quite liked the website layout, things are nicely organized and there is some stuff which I found very interesting specially the Organic jam and candles(On my next shopping list). They included a handwritten note which shows the personal attention to every customer and a go green shopping bag.

What I Ordered:

Soulflower Bite Me Chocolate and Soulflower Hair Conditioner Bar. They added a GoGreen shopping bag :)

Handwritten note, gives a thoughtful touch :)

What I liked:

  1. Vast collection of Organic and Natural products,

  3. According to me it has to be the one stop destination for Organic shopping

What I did nor like:
