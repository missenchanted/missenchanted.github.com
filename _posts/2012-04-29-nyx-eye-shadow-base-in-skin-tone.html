Here is something I have been using for eyes since last 2-3 months. I received this cute tub from Anamika :)

Price: $7.00 for 7g, around Rs.500 at Indian websites

NYX claims: Maximize the intensity, longevity and brilliance of your eye shadow with our eye shadow primer pots. Each of the three NYX Eye Shadow Bases—white for vibrant, pearl for shimmers, or neutral for darker shades—creates a smooth canvas for color, increases its durability and prevents powder fallout.

Ingredients: Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Mineral Oil, Microcrystalline Wax, Octyldodecyl Myristate, Silica (and) Methicone, Nylon-66, Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Dipentaerythrityl Hexahydroxystearate/Hexastearate/Hexarosinate, Ceresin, Sorbitan Sesquioleate, Polyethylene, Fragrance, Butylparaben, Tocopheryl Acetate, and B.H.T. MAY CONTAIN: Titanium Dioxide (CI 77891),Yellow Iron Oxides (CI 77492), Red Iron Oxides (CI 77491), Black Iron Oxides (CI 77499), or Mica ( CI 77019).

Shades available: WHITE, WHITE PEARL, SKIN TONE

My experience: An eye base is a must for you to pop out those colors on the eyes. I am pretty sure, this wont work as concealer for the lids for most Indian skin tones. But as a primer it does its job really well, almost no fallout, colors are surely enhanced and lasts really really long. What I don't like is the thick consistency, you have to really blend it on the eyes, unlike liquid primers.

I had this old buyincoins pallet, whose color would never show up and was lying in one corner of my drawer,

You can make out the major difference this primer does to the shades in the pallet,

Left swatch without NYX base, and right with NYX base

What I like:

  1. Nil Fallout,
  2. Eye color lasts really long,
  3. Color enhancement to a large extent,
  4. NYX products are easily available,
  5. Available in three variants,
  6. One tub, will last really long

What I did not like:

  1. Tub packaging, requires an applicator,
  2. I felt that t could have been a bit better, if it had lighter consistency,
Overall its very decently priced, and does everything expected of an eye base. I totally recommend this. Which is your favorite eye base ?
