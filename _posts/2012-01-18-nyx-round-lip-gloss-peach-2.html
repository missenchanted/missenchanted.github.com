'-' _yoast_wpseo_sitemap-prio: ! '-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- I am just starting to get the knack of NYX products. I first loved their nail colors and now their lip colors too. I grabbed this beauty after going through a lot of swatches. Price: 2$ at cherryculture, I got it for Rs. 158 at 25% discount at stylecraze NYX says: NYX Girls Round Lip Gloss has 36 pretty shades to highlight the sexy pucker of yours. Feels silky smooth, long lasting and the color in the bottle if the color on. My take: I quite like this lip gloss, although its nothing out of the ordinary, but at this price I quite like its texture and color payoff. Its neither sticky, but still manages quite sometime on my lips. What I like:
  1. Very aptly priced,
  2. Non sticky,
  3. Lasts me around 2 hours, but need immediate touch up after lunch breaks,
  4. I like its smell,
  5. Best part the glitter is very fine and doesn't leave behind on lips
What I did not like:
  1. For this particular shade not much pigmentation coverage,
  2. Although its available at selected Indian websites but getting hold of some colors maybe difficult.
I quite like this shade and would definitely some more shades from this range. Have you tried NYX Round lipgloss ?
