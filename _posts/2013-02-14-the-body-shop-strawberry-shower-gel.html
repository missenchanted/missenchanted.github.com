The Body Shop Strawberry Shower Gel

Happy Valentine Day Everyone !!!! No other day would have been better to review a romantic product like The Body shop Strawberry Shower Gel. It is again one of my favorite shower gels, it has this feminine and mild strawberry smell which is not at all overpowering. This shower gel has definitely found place in my good books.

Price: Rs. Rs.395 for 250ml Claims: This soap-free shower gel contains real strawberry seed oil. It has a deliciously sweet scent.

The packaging is standard TBS(The Body shop) packaging. A see though bottle with a sturdy flip cap.

If you love strawberry, then this has to be your staple bath product. Something that smells rich, feminine and yet mild strawberries and neither overpowering or artificial. It lathers really well and does not dry out the skin. Best part is the strawberry fragrance which lingers back on the skin for quite sometime and even the washroom is fragrant like a strawberry garden.

I did not find anything wrong with the product at all. If you do not hate strawberries, then do give this a try.
