Water marble Nail Art

Problems faced:
  1. Nail color should be very runny, a lit bit of viscosity will dampen the marble effect
  2. Some pre work, of cello tape
Whats interesting:
  1. Wow effect
  2. Quick dry
  3. No trouble while doing for left hand :P
Do give it a try once.... here is my try, I did not have enough interesting colors, but could not wait to try :)
