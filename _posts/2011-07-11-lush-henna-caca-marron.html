I bought this block and shared half of it with my mother as she always complains that other Henna's did not work for her properly. Price: Rs. 910 for complete block (This the minimum amount to be bought) My Hair before the application:

Henna contains:

How to use:

  1. I used 1/6th of the block, grated it for my shoulder length hair,
  2. Add Hot water, and make a consistent paste
  3. Apply it covering all hair and for glossy color its advised to cover it wit a wrapping sheet.
  4. Leave it for a couple of hours and wash it with lukewarm water.
  5. Although its not mentioned, but I generally don't shampoo after henna, give it a 24 hour gap, for the color to develop
Hair after wash, What I liked about the product:
  1. It gave shine to my dull hair
  2. Easy to prepare and apply, no hassle of adding extra stuff to the henna
  3. All natural ingredients.
What I did not like:
  1. Leaves behind too Oily hair, need to shampoo before going out. (Too Oily :()
  2. Damn expensive, you have to buy the complete block that's the minimum amount :(
  3. Did not give full coverage to silver hair, left them Orangy as any other Henna does,
I will not buying this again... its too expensive and gives the same result as any other Henna. Even my Mom was not quite happy :( How do you prepare your Henna ?
