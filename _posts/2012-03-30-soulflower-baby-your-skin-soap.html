My Experience: I like Soulflower soaps in general and had some high expectations just because the Soap's name itself promises so many things. Although this soap is very moisturizing but the smell is a turn off. It smells like a concoction of Hot chocolate, yogurt and something weird. My sister was like Ewww :(

I never try soap on my face, but did try this one. It was very foaming yet gentle and did not dry my skin at all, but my nose was not happy.

What I like:

  1. Soap is indeed very moisturizing, it did not even dry the facial skin.. bravo,
  2. Once I reached the inner layer, I could feel a little scrub action, nothing harsh,
  3. Does not melt like crazy, like other soaps,
What I did not like:
  1. Definitely the smell, no one liked it,
  2. Although its easily available online, but in store present only with Shopper stop & Home stop,
This could have been a perfect soap, only if the smell was a bit pleasing. I am not going to purchase this again. There are lot of Soulflower soaps to try out yet. Have you tried "Baby your Skin" soap yet ?
