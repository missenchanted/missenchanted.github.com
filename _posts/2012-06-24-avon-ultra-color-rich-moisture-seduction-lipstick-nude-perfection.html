I have this never ending desire of collecting all natural & nude shades, and it never seems to lessen. Nude perfection from Avon Moisture Rich is a latest addition to my stash. This is my first lipcolor from the range, and I am not disappointed.

Price: Rs.375, but often found with discounts

Avon says: Our most moisture-infused lipstick…for the lushest color you’ve ever known! Up to 500% moisture boost. 1000% full-on sexy lips. Ribbons of rich moisturizers intertwine with seductively smooth color for a sensuous surge of deep hydration.

My Experience: I like it and love it. Its very sheer and works like a nude gloss with subtle shimmer particles. I pair it with Avon glimmerstick nude lipliner and I am good to go. Best part is that its moisturizing and doesn't dry out the lips at all. Now the worst part the stay power, its all gone after snacks, else it stays for around 2 hours.

The colors bullet is really beautiful, with awesome swirls crying out loud... try me ... try me   What I like:
  1. Moisturizing and doesn't dry out at all,
  2. This is a very beautiful nude shade,
  3. Very subtle shimmer, and doesn't need a gloss,
  4. Fits the pockets, I got it for Rs. 229
What I did not like:
  1. The stay power, cant survive the snacks and drink,
Overall its a very pretty lip color in a very sturdy and beautiful packaging. Will surely recommend, if you are looking for a moisturizing color and don't mind the poor stay power. Which is you favorite natural shade ? *This is a scheduled post, me on vacation till 1st July. Take Care.
