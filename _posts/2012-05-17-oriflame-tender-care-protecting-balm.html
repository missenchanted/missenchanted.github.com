'-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- My mom always prepares cream out of natural bees wax and its just the prefect formulae for chapped lips and feet during harsh winters. But I always avoided that cream when stepping out of the house since it felt very heavy and greasy. Bees wax with oil is a perfect blend for all skin woes in winters. Tender care has been a cult favorite from Oriflame, and I have heard such great reviews about this product that I had to buy this. I picked it up for 50% off from March catalogue and just love it.

Price: Rs. 199 for 15ml

Product Description: Oriflame Tender Care is a totally versatile pot of magic, for the whole body. Based on honeycomb extract and vegetable oils. You can use it on sides of eyes, lips, face, cuticles or any area that is dry, sore or roughened to instantly restore softness. Tender Care can be used for cold sores, cuts, grazes, psoriasis, eczma and other skin conditions.

Ingredient List: PETROLATUM, CAPRYLIC/CAPRIC TRIGLYCERIDE, PARAFFINUM LIQUIDUM, CERA ALBA, PARAFFIN, ACETYLATED LANOLIN, CETYL ALCOHOL, TOCOPHERYL ACETATE, PROPYLPARABEN

My experience: I just love the packaging, its cute, sleek and classy. This is one versatile product can be used for any skin probelm likes chapped and rough skin. I used it as a lip balm under my lip colors, and its felt very light and gets absorbed very fast. Best use for me has to be as cuticle cream, it softened them in a day and I just cant stop using.

What I like:

  1. Its a versatile product for all skin problems,
  2. Very cute and chick packaging,
  3. Very light texture and gets absorbed very easily,
Although its an all rounder but I feel the quantity is quite less, and as lip balm its average as I have met better lip balms. I would surely recommend this cream, its a must for the handbag for those cuticle and lips emergencies.
