Maybelline colossal kajal

Its hard to review a product sometimes, specially when its loved and adored by many. Maybelline colossal kajal is favorite of so many people I personally know, and me being a kajal addict had to pick it up. Was I impressed..hmm.. difficult question that is.... read on, Price: Rs. 150
Claims: Our deepest black pigments + unique tip deliver the blackest black shine ever. Color glides on smooth and so intense. Formula enriched with Vitamin E soothes the eyes. Never before 6H of smudge resistant wear. Our exclusive color fix formula guarantees intense color results that won't smudge, budge or fade. No more touchups.
The packaging is a retractable pencil, which everyone loves. I like the fact that its really black and does not smudge for hours. One thing to notice here, you have to let the kajal set for a few minutes first before it becomes smudge proof.
My sister loves this kajal, because she finds the Lakme eye conic eye liner a bit uncomfortable on her waterline. But since I do not have sensitive eyes I did not face any problem.
But I have complaints too, I think that the texture should be a bit more creamy as I find myself tugging my lids sometime.
Now I tell you why reviewing Maybelline colossal kajal was a trick task..... since for the price I think this product is good but I just wish the texture was a bit more soft.
I would definitely recommend the product if you looking for something smudge and water proof in the price range. College going girls and teens would love it.
Also do pick this up if you have sensitive eyes, as its safe on waterline too.
