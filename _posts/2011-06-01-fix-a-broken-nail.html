We cant avoid Kitchen work, Â Gym, household cleaning and then you get a chipped nail. Months of efforts to keep nails long and healthy goes down the drain :(. Good news, we can fix those nails with items in our cupboard. You need:
  1. Tea bag,
  2. Glue(Prefer nail glue)
  3. Scissors, clipper
  4. Cotton, nail paint remover
  5. Nail file
Steps to mend that nail:
  1. Remove nail paint, and clean the nail with soap and water
  2. Take a tea bag and cut it into the shape of your nail ,
  3. Apply nail glue and press the tea bag piece on the nail with the use of a clipper, leave some extra ends at the nail top and fold it inside the nail, specially at the chipped corner.
  4. I have used quickfix as it was an emergency, but avoid using it, it stains the nail.
  5. Once dried, use a file to shape the nail properly and remove bumps.
  6. Now your nail is all set and repaired. avoid sheer nail colors till the nail grows properly and the chipped portion can be removed.
Take care:
  1. A mended nail is still week, so use it with care,
  2. Use a nail glue, if not available use a clean color nail polish it will do the work,
  3. Use opaque colors for sometime :)
