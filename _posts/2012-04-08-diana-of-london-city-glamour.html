Diana of London grabbed my attention, after I read about them online. This small bottle has a lot to promise, free of harsh chemicals, fast dry, long lasting and enriched with vitamin C and Calcium.

Price: Rs. 210, but you can grab these at major discounts from medplusbeauty.com and stylecraze.com

About City Glamour:

FAST DRYING, HIGH-SHINE WITH UV FILTER. 7 DAY STAY

State-of-the-art nail enamel, High-shine finish with an instant stroke of glamour, irresistible colors go on smoother and faster without chipping and streaking, set in 60 seconds, Toluene,DBP, Camphor & Formaldehyde free

Available in 67 shades, you can check them out here

My Experience: When I first saw the bottle, I found the packaging not quite impressive, but I must admit, I liked the brush. Long bristles give an ease when applying the color on long nails. This particular shade Soft Brown - 02 gives an opaque layer in 2 coats. Definitely fast dry formula free from Toluene,DBP, Camphor & Formaldehyde. The below pictures are taken after 3 days of application and shows minimal chipping.

What I liked:

  1. Toluene, DBP, Camphor & Formaldehyde Free.
  2. Good Lasting power, as you can see in the pictures minimal chipping in 3 days,
  3. I quite like the brush, helps in giving a smooth application,
  4. Reasonably priced for the quantity and quality (Major discounts online),
  5. Enriched with Vit E and calcium, but thats actually hard for me to validate (needs a lot of time),
What I did not like:
  1. Packaging I would say, it does not justify its quality in my opinion,
  2. May not be available in stores for all cities (Present in all major and metro cities), but that when online shops and discounts come in handy

I would definitely recommend this to all. Grab them at major 32% discount on MedPlusBeauty.
