Jabong is a household name when we think about online shopping, thanks to the catchy TV ads. I have already shopped with them twice and this was my third order with them and I am a satisfied customer. Jabong has made its mark and stands out when compared to its contemporaries, for its unique customer service and delivery... read on to know more..

I was impressed with the large inventory and categories displayed, they have some great collection of shoes and bags. When I had placed my first order, I wasn't aware of their open and check delivery system. It was a pleasant surprise, but unluckily I did not find the stuff too good so I returned it back. It was so simple, no hassle, no fuss and the order was returned.

You will be amazed at the large category of products from Shoes to bags and Home accessories to Kids Toys. They come up with some amazing sales seriously unto 70% from time to time. Like every other girl, I never miss out on their shoes and Bags collection,   What makes Jabong stands out from others:
  1. A very very large collection of shoes and bags... and other stuff too,
  2. Very carefully planned and easy to navigate website
  3. Very prompt customer service,
  4. Unique open and check policy before delivery,
  5. Customer friendly return policy, they pick up order from your shipping address,
Scope of improvement:
  1. I would suggest them to bring back the sold out stuff back, they really had some cute pieces which are still waiting for return in my Wishlist
I ordered a Blue Holli bag, and within 48 hours the order reached me, I checked and accepted it. I am really happy with this cute looking wallet,
Thumbs up to their customer service, tracking methods and product delivery/returns. Totally recommend shopping with them http://www.jabong.com, do check out their collection. * I was provided a gift voucher for purchase but that has not affected my opinion.. promise.
