Okie Dokie Artichokie, the name itself is so catchy (Reminds me of phrases from Masterchef Australia), I might have just picked it up for that. But I received it from our lovely Ana from wiseshe, who needs no introduction in the blogging world. Okie Dokie lets get on the review,

The pot looks really cute, and the color just stands out in the pot, but its just okies dokie

   Price: Rs. 500 (approximately) and $6 in International market

 Claims: The Lip Lacquer Pot is a portable lip gloss in a cute and sexy container easy for on-the-go application and touch-ups! This high-impact lip lacquer delivers a gorgeous shine. Comes in 12 different colors from sultry red to nudes and shimmery hues.

   

 My experience: This indeed is a beautiful looking pot. The color is just lovely with golden subtle shimmer, but its on a shear side, and may not work on pigmented lips alone. You need a base color beneath to build up the shade. Has average lasting power of around 2 hours. Formula is neither moisturizing, nor drying. What I hate the most I need to carry around a brush along with it.

  What I like:
  1. Subtle shimmer and shine, gives a really good glossy effect,
  2. Very sturdy packaging, dropped it so many times and not even a scratch,
  3. As gloss the lasting power is good, but alone not good,
  4. Real cheap, if bought in US
What I did not like:
  1. Absence of a lip brush,
  2. This particular shade is more on shear side,
Overall its an average product(atleast this shade), I am surely not buying this for the Indian price, but would love to try some more shades, if I can manage them from US. Have you tried the NYX Lip Lacquer Pot?
