Enchantess March Box

This month I skipped Vellvette box, no doubt they were sending good products but I was tired of toner samples and products that I might never end up using. So I took a break from them and subscribed for Enchantess March Samples. So here is my Pink potli that I received today,

You can check my previous Enchantess Box here 

Price: Rs.399 + free shipping for 3 sample products

This is what came in my box for this month,

  1. 3 samples,
  2. Discount vouchers,
  3. Inside Salon and Spa magazine

A little more info about the products in the box,

  1. Calvin Klein Lip Pot - Full Size
  2. Clean Dry Shampoo - Full Size
  3. Snan Neem and Turmeric mild soap - 30gm sample

A closer look at the samples. I was just wondering how sample services provide luxury full size samples. Since none of the products have any expiry and manufacture date mentioned on them. I checked for Calvin Klein Lip Pot on Cosmetic Calulator and since the manufacture date is April'2011 I am left to think has it really expired ?

I hope not because its a really beautiful shade and am quite happy with it.

Overall I am quite happy with my Enchantess March month box, and planning to subscribe for April too.

I am still looking for the Calvin Klein Lip Pot shelf life on internet and no success yet.
