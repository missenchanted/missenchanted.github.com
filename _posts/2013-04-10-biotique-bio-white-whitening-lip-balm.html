Biotique Bio White whitening Lip Balm Review

Fruit has been the elixir of life since the beginning of time, rich in vitamins, nutrients and skin brighteners. This de-pigmentation balm visibly lightens lips for a fairer skin tone.
Price: Rs.99 for 4.1gm Ingrediants: Angur(Vitis Vinifera) 2.0%, Vach (Acorus Calamus) 1.5%, Mulethi (Glycyrrhiza glabra) 2.0%, Badam(Prunus amygaldus) 1.5%, Surajmukhi (Helianthus annus) 2.5%, Kusumbhi (Carthamus tinctorius) 4.0%, Til (Sesamum indium) 4.5%, Erandi (Riinus communis) 12.0%, Marham Base Biotique Bio White whitening Lip Balm  is a tinted balm, which is not glossy and rather looks natural on the lips. That's the only thing I liked about it. Regarding moisturization, I find myself applying this every hour. It wont work on dry and chapped lips and did nothing to lighten the lips or fade pigmentation. Lack of SPF is whats shocks me more. For me its a total waste of money and I won't recommend this to anyone. What I liked:
  1. The tint, it hides pigmentation and looks very natural on the lips,
What I did not like:
  1. The packaging is very clumsy,
  2. Lacks SPF,
  3. Does nothing to lighten the lips,
  4. Not very moisturizing
Tips to lighten lip balm,
  • Stop chewing your lips, some people do that and its the biggest culprit for darker and pigmented lips,
  • Use lip balms with SPF
  • Scrub your lips with 2 drops of olive oil and a pinch of sugar, it works wonders on dry lips.
I won't be purchasing this again for sure, due to lack of SPF and the unfulfilled claims of whitening the lips. You can check some other good lip balms here, Biotique Bio Almond overnight therapy lip balm review MUA Love Hearts Lip Balm Maybelline Baby Lips Lipbalm
