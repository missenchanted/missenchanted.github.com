Ok now you will ask how about if there is no Kajal..... aha then I wont go out :P he he I happen to pick up Avon extra lasting kajal & smudger a month back when it was launched. Price: Rs.229 for 1.14g, but I got it for 159 Claims: 
  • Improved formula with smoother texture for easier application,
  • Soft creme formula that lines and defines without pulling and tugging,
  • Special oil absorbing formula absorbs excess oil for longer wear,
  • Use the smudger for easily smooth blend
My take: Its a beautiful pencil made of wood, which is not very smooth while sharpening and a lot of product gets wasted. Its not fully smudgeproof. The shade is not the darkest that I have ever used but I really like the smudger part its good quality and easily gives a smokey look. What I like:
  1. Very decently priced and easily available through Avon consultants,
  2. Pencil and smudger in one , quite handy and with caps on each side its travel friendly,
  3. Lasts quite long on the lid without fading for like 5-6 hours,
  4. Good pigmentation though not the darkest
What I did not like:
  1. Sharpening is not that smooth and some part of it gets wasted,
  2. Has a powdery texture instead of a creamy/waxy for a Kajal,
Its a great pencil for a quick smokey look, but as a kajal I don't prefer this as the texture is quite powdery, not very intense on waterline and for watery eyes it will smudge.  But for this price its a good looking pencil  for your pouch and comes in handy for a quick smokey look . Do you like smokey looks ?
