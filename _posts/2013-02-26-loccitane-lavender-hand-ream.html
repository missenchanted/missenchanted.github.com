L'occitane Lavender Hand Cream

The only product that has an actual Lavender fragrance. Best of all the Lavender based products that I have tried so far. L'occitane Lavender Hand Cream smells heavenly and does decent job as hand cream. Price: Rs.  for 30ml Claims: Enriched with shea butter and lavender essential oil from Haute-Provence, this smooth, light-textured cream gently helps to nourish the hands leaving them softly perfumed. A beautiful tube in silver and purple which is heavenly fragrant. The texture is thick but gets absorbed very easily without any greasiness. Its moisturizing but not sufficient for dry hands or hands that need some extra care. What I loved:
  1. Beautiful and travel friendly packaging,
  2. Absolutely heavenly fragrance, which lasts long on the hands,
  3. Totally not sticky
  4. Gets absorbed very easily,
What I did not like:
  1. No SPF,
  2. Not much for dry cuticle and excessive dry hands
No doubt its pricey but its worth the price. Girls who love Lavender, believe me this a must have for you.
