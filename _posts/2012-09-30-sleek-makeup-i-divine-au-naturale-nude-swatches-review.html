I recently picked up the the neutral pallets from Sleek Makeup, and no doubt that these babies look delicious but sadly I was a bit disappointed with the pigmentation of at least half of them.

Price: $9

  Packaging is good and comes with a full size mirror.. bravo for that and a double ended applicator.. not so much bravo for that. The darker shades are so much beautifully pigmented, one swipe and you get complete coverage, but the lighters ones just did not obey and so I had to use a primer and use Flash photography to show them to you guys. Swatches: I have oily eye lids, and matte shades stay just for 4-5 hours, whereas the darker shades last much longer. If you want to pick up this pallet for the softer shades on the upper row, I won't recommend these but aha… the lower row is just beautiful. So its a catch you see… but will do the swatch and review for Oh So special very soon so that you can decide between the two. Happy weekend everyone!!!!!
