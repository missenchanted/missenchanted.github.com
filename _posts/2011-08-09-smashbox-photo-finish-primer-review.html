In my honest opinion, I feel this is a keeper . However, I have used it only on rare occasions (wedding or parties) for myself as well as the bride-to-be as its freaking expensive and cant afford to waste the product . This is a beauty must-have for a photo-ready face . This can be purchased at strawberrynet. The product itself is the texture of a thick gel , it gives a velvety feel when rubbed between the palms. It gives a smooth canvas for applying foundation or powder. The makeup never slides or disintegrates even in hot humid Indian weather and gives a right amount of glow-from-within look.
