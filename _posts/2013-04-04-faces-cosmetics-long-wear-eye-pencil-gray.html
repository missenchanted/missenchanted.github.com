Faces Cosmetics Long Wear Eye Pencil Gray

I am a big Faces Cosmetics Long Wear Eye Pencil fan. I was on a lookout for a perfect grey for subtle eye and hence picked up this pencil. You can see the complete swatch gallery here Faces Long Wear Eye Pencil... Price: Rs.349 (earlier same pencil was priced Rs.250) I love,
  1. Its creamy and just glides without tugging,
  2. Intensely pigmented,
  3. Long lasting on the lids,
  4. Can be blended on the lids when freshly applied,
Where I struggled,
  1. Smudges on waterline after 2-3 hours, so as kohl not that really effective,
  2. I could never get perfect tip after sharpening,
