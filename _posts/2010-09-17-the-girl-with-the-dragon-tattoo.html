The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo by Stieg Larsson

Stieg Larsson brings back the Sidney Sheldon style of writing with The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo. I had heard some great reviews from friends who have watched movies based on the trilogy. The book also ranks as a bestseller.

I agree that the story had its bit of suspense and thriller. But this book failed to impress me. As a mystery novel the Author does not involve its reader with the story and no scope is left for us to make a quick guess, and the climax is dropped on to the readers.

I was intrigued with the plot, but I did not like the style of writing. People who still like Sidney for (Female protagonist, Crime, Sex and Betrayal) will enjoy this book.

I would suggest this book only if you like Sidney Sheldon, I personally had left this genre long back during college days.
