Avon has recently a new line of nail colors, really affordable, great quality, quantity and glossy finish. Although shades are limited but each one is a steal,

Price: Rs. 119 (Mostly on Discounts) Claims:
  • Salon-quality, high shine formula lasts up to 10 days,
  • Fortified with acrylic precision brush,

My Experience: Love the assortment of colors, and picked up a couple of them, love the finish,

Aqua Fantasy is a cool Minty shade with a subtle pearl finish, and Loving Lavender is a beautiful Lilac shade with a glossy finish.

Regarding 10 days claim, I did not have the patience to try it, I was bored in 5 days and changed the color. Almost no chipping with a top coat, but I will surely test and update the claim,

Although both of them are from same collection, but has different length of brush, strange right ?

Finally we have an affordable, yet quality nail paints in India. I am picking up all the shades....
