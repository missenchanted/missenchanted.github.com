  • Water-resistant formula ensures no smudging or smearing.
  • Deep intense color that does not fade.
What I like about the product:
  1. Quantity lasts long, really long
  2. Color is rich jet black, even a single swipe is enough to reach it,
  3. Nice packaging
  4. Cost effective
What I dont like about the product:
  1. It dries very slowly, if not careful leave marks and has to be cleaned with a Q tip,
  2. I hate the applicator brush, very flimsy, I use eye liner brush to get that strong line,
  3. When I splash my face with water, I have to be careful not to swipe my eyes,
  4. It smudges, but then with this price its too much to expect,
Will I buy again: No, for a first timer is good, although rich in color and low in price, an eyeliner is expected to be smudge proof, water proof and dry quickly and not forget a good quality applicator. Which eye liner are you carrying ?
