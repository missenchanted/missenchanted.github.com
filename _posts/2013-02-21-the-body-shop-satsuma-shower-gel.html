The Body Shop Satsuma shower gel

Close your eyes and feel standing in an orange orchard with pure fragrance and sunlight ...Nope you did not feel that... its because have not met the Satsuma range from The Body Shop. Its my favorite shower gel of all times and even though I keep on trying new brands, but I always have one bottle of The Body Shop Satsuma shower gel with me. Price: Rs. Rs.395 for 250ml Claims: This soap-free shower gel contains zesty satsuma essential oil and has a refreshing citrus scent. Soap-free Lather-rich Refreshing citrus scent Comes in a standard The body shop packaging in a see through bottle and a flip cap. The gel seems like sun light captured in a limited space. Its orangish golden.. if I could describe that way. It has a zesty yet sweet orange smell, which does not seem artificial. Lathers beautifully and leaves back squeaky clean skin. The fragrance lingers back on the skin and the washroom too. Sometimes I come back from office after hours and my washroom still smells like orange. Few drops are enough to lather nicely, so one bottle would easily last around 3 months. I have nothing negative to talk about this product and just love it to bits. This is a highly recommended product, and everyone should at least take a sniff at the TBS counter and I am pretty sure you will end end up bringing this beauty home. Have your tried The Body Shop Satsuma shower gel.. if not you are missing something !!!
