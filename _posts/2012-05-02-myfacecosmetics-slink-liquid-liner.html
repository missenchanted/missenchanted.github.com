 "Kohl, Eyeliner and Mascara are a religion"

And I am from the above sect, who just can't step out of the house without the three. Crisp black lines and defined eyes add a special touch. I am a fan of Defined eyes rather than volume and drama, although I do try them out sometimes.

Price: £10.99 for 2.5ml Comes in one variants Paint it black MyFaceCosmetics claims:
    • Matte black liquid liner
    • Create dramatic definition that lasts all day; won’t smudge or flake
    • Pair with black ice Blingtone to create a modern, monochromatic look
    • Draw on your own one-of-a-kind accessories, give your tattoos a new splash of colour, or send an instant message.

My Experience: The ingredients are not mentioned, neither I could get them from the website. Packaging is sleek and chic. What I like the most is the brush, handle its long enough to give a steady hand and a small brush tip to give precise application. The formula is instant dry, smudge proof and lasts me throughout the day. Its intense black and gives a matte black after drying. But its not waterproof, so I have to be careful while splashing water on my eyes.

 

 Its intense black and one of the beautiful black I have seen.

What I Like:

  1. How intense black this is,
  2. Handle is long enough to give a steady hand and a small brush tip to give precise application,
  3. Gives a matte finish,
  4. Instant dry and smudge proof,

What I did not like:

  1. I expect it to be water proof,
  2. Availability is be an issue, you can get it online from here

I totally love this for the finish and the brush, the matte look is way better than the Lakme liquid eyeliner. I just asked my friend to grab a few for me when returning back.
