So I started with the Karma soap. My first requirement to the SA was long lingering fragrance, and she instantly picked up this cherry colored soap with a strong fragrance. Price : Rs. 485 for 100gm Product description: Top up your Karma quotient with a quick wash during the day. Buy a big chunk, slice some off for the washbasin and the kitchen sink then keep the rest for the shower. Product contains: Coconut oil, Perfume, Patchouli oil, Sweet Orange oil, Lavandin oil, Pine oil, Lemongrass oil, Elemi oil, Glycerine, EDTA, Gardenia extract, Citral, Geraniol, Citronellol, Limonene, Linalool, Color 18050 Shelf life: 14 months What I Love about the product:
  1. Its smell, and yeah it does stay for a real long time
  2. Content look all safe, but then that what we expect of LUSH
What I dont like about the product:
  1. Quite expensive :(
  2. 100 gm will last around 20 days
  3. Although it includes a mix of oils, but leaves my skin dry
  4. I feel its like evaporating from the shelf like camphor :( :(
Will not be buying this again, its quite expensive, fast exhausting and very drying. Which LUSH Soap is your favorite ?
