I recently picked up my first LUSH fresh face mask and just loving it. Love lettuce does look like shredded lettuce in a clay base but did I see lettuce in the ingredients, nope surprisingly I did not. But still no doubt its a great mask for normal to oily skin like mine.

It has grounded almonds hence feels a little gritty and works as mask cum scrub. Take a small quantity, mix it with water and leave on for 7-10 min, scrub it slowly out and wash with warm water. It feels fresh and gives a glow, which does last for like a day. I wont say this does wonders, I would say its an average mask for normal-oily skin, but since its completely natural and fresh, I like it. Also the scrub is very gentle and can be used everyday. Major disadvantage is short life span of 2 weeks and it needs to be stored in refrigerator. What I like:
  1. Fresh and natural ingredients,
  2. Works as mask cum scrub,
  3. Does not dry up the skin, leave behind soft and glowing skin,
  4. I quite like the smell n color, but thats just me I think,
What I did not like:
  1. I wish they give masks in lesser quantities, since life span is so short,
  2. Has to be stored in refrigerator,
  3. Glow does not last long
I love LUSH products, but the policy of minimum purchase should not stand for masks I believe, its quite hard to finish them off in 2 weeks, and who uses masks daily.
