Its Sunday afternoon, and I am feeling lazy. Nothing new about it, but I wanted to share the reviews about Diana of London Dress your eyes Mascara,which I have been using for quite sometime now.

Price: Rs. 595 for 8ml

Claims: Non smudging formula sensational volume without clumping surprising length & perfect separation.

New Waterproof Formula -Promises day long wear without imperfections Formula Studied -To increase and wide at the most the lashes, giving it thickness and volume Special Polymers -Gives you an intense black color & helps maintain the styling of eyelashes.

Ingredients:

My experience: I will start with the packaging, nothing unusual or new about it, but I do like the pink  color & graphics. Brush is dense and is designed to give volume. It does give a slight volume without any clumping, but gives me dry looking eyelashes. Best thing is that its waterproof, but I  hope it added a bit of shine to my lashes. Oh did I forget to mention its quick dry, and thats what I need the most in morning.

What I like:

  1. It's waterproof, and doesn't smudge,
  2. Does provides with slight volume,
  3. Does not clump,
  4. Quick dry

What I did not like:

  1. I did not find it much lengthening,
  2. Since it dries matte, gives a very dry and brittle look,
If this wasn't a matte finish, I might have picked it up again, but I prefer the Diana of London Movie Magic Mascara for its more defined looks. You can try this for a matte finish, else pair it up with DOL Aqua lift gel Masacara for the glossy finish. Have you tried the Diana of London products ?
