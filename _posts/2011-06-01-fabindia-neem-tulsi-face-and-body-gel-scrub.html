Price: Rs. 225 for 100g Product boasts of: Body and gel face scrub, especially formulated for daily use is made with natural active ingredients. It gently exfoliates the skin leaving you feeling refreshed and invigorated all day long. Product contains: Purified water, Propylene glycol, Walnut shell powder, Carbopol 940, Aloe vera extract, neem leaves, Phenoxyethanol, Lactic acid, Tulsi oil, Allantoin, Neem Oil Directions for use: Apply a scoop of the scrub to wet skin and massage all over in circular motion, focusing on rough skin areas. Rinse and clean throughly. Pros:
  1. First thing that comes to mind is neem and margo smell, but this product has pleasant fragrance,
  2. Scrub is quite gentle and can be used on face daily,
  3. It has neem oil, tulsi oil and aloe vera you can feel them on your skin after the wash. Skin feels smooth and soft.
  4. Did not break me out.
  5. Ingredients look natural and very less chemical (Hope so)
Cons:
  1. Too gentle to be a body scrub (Will last 3-4 times if used so)
  2. I am not a tub packing fan.
