I had been looking for a perfect day cream for myself with SPF. I have always struggled with SPF's they tend to bring down my color and most of them feel sticky(I have oily and acne prone skin). I ordered a 20g small tube of this from healthkart for trial. Price: Rs. 90 after online discount Contains : What I like about the product:
  1. Definitely enhanced my skin tone, and have been receiving compliments lately :)
  2. SPF is enough for slight exposure under sun, but for longer duration or beach choose higher SPF,
  3. It does moisturize my skin, but for very dry skin it wont be enough. For winters opt for Lotion
  4. Comes in a smaller tube so, easy to carry around in a bag
  5. I am using my 2nd tube and it has not broken me out even once.
