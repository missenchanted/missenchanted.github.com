"Dark Angels" reminds me of Dan Brown's books, full of thrill and plots.

But this dark angel is very simple in looks and stands for its claims. Its a great cleanser exfoliator for oily skin like mine, and highly recommended ...

Price: Rs. 700 for 100gm

Claims: Dark Angels is the nemesis of all blemishes, breakouts and blocked pores. Our soft black scrub was conceived when we combined the exfoliating power of Angels on Bare Skin and the sebum balancing abilities of Coal Face. The result? A velvety cleanser, sprinkled with black sugar and powdered charcoal to gently exfoliate and cleanse away excess oils. We add antimicrobial rhassoul mud and vitamin-rich cold pressed organic avocado oil to soften, moisturize and nourish skin, as well as sandalwood and rosewood oils to soothe.

Contains:

My Experience: This cleanser is very granular and can be abrasive if not used carefully, removes dead skin and oil, leaving behind soft skin and glowing skin. I prefer to use it as mask and cleanser both. This one actually helped in reducing acne size to and extent.

How to use: Dampen your face with warm water. Place a pea-sized chunk in the palm of your hand and add water to create a paste. Cleanser will remain fresh for up to three months.

I prefer to use it 2-3 times a week both as mask and cleanser.

Highly recommended to battle out oily skin woes. This is the prefect cleanser for me removes dead skin, oil and gives that natural glow and soft skin, but do check out first as some people can find it a bit abrasive. A less abrasive alternative has to be the LUSH Coal Face.
