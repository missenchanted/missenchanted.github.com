I had never used tinted balm except TBS Satsuma Lip Balm which has glitter(review later). On my last visit to health and glow I picked up this balm, just for its cute packing.

 

Price: Rs. 60 for 8 gm Product boasts of: Candylicious care for your lovely lips. With SPF 15 for sun protection, almond oil to moisturize and vitamin E to enrich, it leaves your lips inviting, berry-red and sweet. Product contains: Octyl methoxycinnamate, Benzophenone 3, Beeswax, Jojoba oil, Sweet Almond oil, Tocopheryl Acetate(Vitamin E) None of the ingredient looks scary My experience: It has a sweet fragrance and taste :P. Gives nice tint to my lips. What I like:
  1. Available inÂ  Classic vanilla, Tangy orange, Blackcurrant burst and Plum sparkle.
  2. SP15 included.
  3. Pleases fragrance and taste.
  4. Attractive and cute sliding pack.
  5. I liked the red tint it gives to the Lips.
What I did not like:
  1. Application of balm is bit messy, you end up getting a red tint on your fingers and need to be washed.
  2. Not quite hygienic I would add.
  3. No use for Dry and chapped lips, very less moisturization
My Verdict: I liked the product, but will I buy it again maybe. I am quite happy with my Nivea, Nuetregena balms. But I might give other variants a try for fun.
