Wishing you all a very very Belated Happy Diwali..may the coming year brings you lots of happiness, good health and love. I had a great Diwali, decorated my house, made rangoli and burnt very few crackers and almost became deaf for few minutes.

I wore the NYX powder blush this Diwali and was surprised that I haven't swatched it here before,

Price: Rs. 520

Definitely more on the light pink side than peach, so the shade name is a misnomer, don't fall for it. As the NYX powder blush in Silky Rose(Reviewed here) this one is also a matte shade which is very soft and easy to blend. Average stay power of around 4-5 hours on me.  

This one is strictly for fairer skin, dusky beauties will find it ash like and will totally wash them out.

NYX powder blushes are a good place to start and experiment with colors, only if we could get them for US prices in India. Why everything has to quadruple before selling in India... thats certainly not fair.
