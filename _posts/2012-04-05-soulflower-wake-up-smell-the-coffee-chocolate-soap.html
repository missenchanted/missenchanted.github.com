I was all excited today, when I saw a picture of mine in India Today. We just happened to be at a place at that time :)

I have this big pile of soap collection now, and I am just loving it. I have been using this soap for last 15 days, and not quite impressed. Read on...

Price: Rs. 250 for 150gm (Got it for Rs.170 from FnU)
About product: Start your day with the rich scent of dark chocolate & freshly ground coffee bean. This soap invigorates,  exfoliates, moisturises ,deodorises, protects against sun’s UV rays & of course cleans.First scrub your skin smooth with the coffee side & then wash with the chocolate side to leave skin soft & silky.

Use on dry to normal skin. Not suitable for irritated skin.

Ingredients: Fresh ground coffee, cocoa powder, real dark chocolate, cocoa butter, Vitamin E, glycerin, titanium dioxide, aqua, saponified oils of olive, coconut, castor, palm & palm kernel.

My Experience:

"Dil ke armaan aansuon mein beh gaye"

The minutes I read grounded coffee, I knew I need this, but since the day I have started using this soap, I am lost can't find a trace of coffee anywhere.  I initially thought since I cut this into 4 pieces coffee part must be still in box, but after trying all... nope nothing there. This one does smell like chocolate very similar to Bite me Chocolate soap. It has very gentle exfoliaters, don't realize when I use it, and is kind of mild moisturizing as well. But again where is the coffee ??

What I like:

  1. Chocolate smell

What I did not like:

  1. Not a trace of coffee :(
  2. Mild moisturizing (I have super dry skin),

I won't recommend this soap, to any coffee lover, but yes if you like chocolate you can give this a try or better try this one. I wish this soap had that strong punch of coffee, this soap couls have been the best soap ever and ever.
