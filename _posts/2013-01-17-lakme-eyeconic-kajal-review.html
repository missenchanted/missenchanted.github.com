'-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- Last year was all about so many product release from Lakme, Maybelline, Revlon etc. But one that caught my attention the most was the Lakme Eyeconic range, I have been using them for the last 2-3 month and am highly impressed. Price: Rs. 199 Claims:
  • Smudge proof
  • Waterproof
  • Lasts 10hours

Ok all the claims are actually true, and I am currently loving this more than any black eyeliner that I have in my kit for the same price range. The retractable packing and new looks from Lakme certainly defines this new range.

Its soft and does not tug and pull the eye. Intensely pigmented and a true black. Lasts me 8-9 hours on the eyelid, but on my waterline it stays for like 4-5hours and then slightly starts to fade away, without making me look like a panda

Highly impressed with Lakme for providing such good quality Kohl in the price range. Ok do I hear... how this fares against Maybelline Collosal Kajal ?? Maybelline kajal does not stand any chance infront of Lakme eye conic range. I have had hard time getting a true black on my waterline with Maybelline one and it always fails. Slight smudge test, and as you see it does budge slightly but still I am ok with it. Lakme has also launched eyeconic Mascara to go with this and that too is my good list. More on that in my next post. If you are a kohl addict, or even if you like it sometimes only.. Lakme eyeconic Kajal is for you and everyone. I highly recommend this.
