Price: Rs.99 Product boasts of : Lightweight soufflé makeup, perfect for everyday wear.
  1. Enriched with sunscreens and cucumber extracts.
  2. 100% water based formula is lightweight so skin does not feel weighed down by extra layers
  3. Souffle form allows for easy application to give skin a natural, flawless look
Consistency: Creamy and non flowing

Pros:
  • Good shades for Indian skin tones,
  • Blends easily,
  • Dirt cheap,
Cons:
  • Not suitable for dry skin, dries off very quickly and leaves behind creases if left alone.
  • Does not giving coverage and finishing of a foundation.
  • Does not give that glow as expected of foundation, but with Rs. 99 not much can be expected.
  • Just touching the face with a dry tissue gets a stain.
  • Inclusion of SPF , but number not mentioned
Will I buy again: No, it was just meant to get me hands on experience of foundation usage, but did not deliver anything.
