I am sure many of you must be aware of Liz Earle's cult favorite Cleanse & Polish hot cloth cleanser. I have been using it for the last 2 weeks and totally loving it. Its a magical product and my skin care will always be incomplete without it.

About Liz Earle : 

Price: £13.75 for 100ml pump starter kit.

Ingredients: Aqua (water), Caprylic/capric triglyceride, Theobroma cacao (cocoa) seed butter, Cetearyl alcohol, Cetyl esters, Sorbitan stearate, Polysorbate 60, Glycerin, Cera alba (beeswax), Propylene glycol, Humulus lupulus (hops) extract, Panthenol, Rosmarinus officinalis (rosemary) extract, Anthemis nobilis (chamomile) extract, Eucalyptus globulus (eucalyptus) oil, Limonene, Citric acid, Sodium hydroxide, Phenoxyethanol, Benzoic acid, Ethylhexylglycerin, Dehydroacetic acid, Polyaminopropyl biguanide

 How I use it: 

  1. Apply one pump of the cream over dry face, and massage in circular motion for a minute,
  2. Rinse the Muslin cloth in hot water and wring it out for any dripping water,
  3. Gently wipe off the cream, taking care around eye and nose,
  4. Splash cold water, to close the pores,
  5. Since I have oily skin, I stop here but dry skin people should go ahead with a light moisturizer.
  6. Rinse the muslin cloth and dry it in natural air. Use mild soap sometime to clean it.
Awards:
My Experience: I have super oily skin, which is quite prone to breakouts if not given proper care. Cleansing to me always meant using a foaming face wash which will keeps oil under control. But this product changed my belief, that cleansing creams are not meant for oily skin. This is a 4-in-1 product for cleanser, exfoliater, moisturizer and makeup remover. The Muslin cloths that come with it are real good quality and just enough to exfoliate without being abrasive. It removes every spec of makeup and dirt specially eye makeup.

I always had issue with kajal, water proof mascaras and eye liners,even after eye makeup removers and cleansers I could never get rid of the black specs below my eyes, but this leaves me with squeaky clean eyes :)

What I like about the product:

  1. Is not at all greasy and suitable for all skin types,
  2. 4-in-1 for for cleanser, exfoliater, moisturizer and makeup remover,
  3. Slight moisturizing and did not break me out, but dry skin will need some moisture,
  4. My skin felt smoother, softer and glowing after 2-3 use itself,
  5. All safe and natural ingredients,
  6. Travel friendly and comes in a very cute green bag.
  7. The packaging pump bottle is recyclable,
  8. Muslin cloths provided are good quality and they exfoliate the skin very gently, unless it is rubbed with hard hands,
  9. Not tested on Animals.
  10. Oh, I forgot to mention, I love the eucalyptus smell it has.

What I did not like:

  1. Availability is the big issue, and one will have to pay for shipping.

I have already asked my friend to get me 2 kits. Its now part of my skin regime and I don't know how will I manage without this product. I highly recommend this, do try it out and you won't regret :)
