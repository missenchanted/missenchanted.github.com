'-' _yoast_wpseo_sitemap-prio: ! '-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- I have always been trying to find the perfect moisturizer for my face. I hate thick lotions for my face as they leave a sticky, greasy layer and make my already oily skin look even oilier. Most of the lotions that I have tried have given me breakouts and thus, I have broken up with them. Well, I bought the Cetaphil Moisturizing lotion when I saw it in a pharmacy, lying on a rack, beside my favorite Cetaphil cleanser. Without any thoughts, I bought it and have been trying it out since 3 months. Price- Rs. 200 for 100ml Claims- Moisturizing Lotion specifically formulated to soothe chronically dry skin. Non-comedogenic Cetaphil® Moisturizing Lotion has a non-greasy formulation that is mild, non-irritating and long lasting. This gentle moisturizing formula contains emollients and humectants that bind moisture to the skin. This fragrance-free moisturizer contains no lanolins or parabens. Ingredients: -  

My Experience:- This lotion is quite thick in consistency, is white and fragrance free. The lotion is so thick that I have to shake the bottle very hard to take out the lotion. I tried it on my face one day and found out that it simply makes my face very oily and greasy. I immediately understood that it is not for my sensitive oily skin. I tried it out on my neck, knees, and elbows and found out that it makes them soft and supple. I used it for my feet as well and it works perfectly for them. I got slight cracks on my heels and within a few days only, I saw that the cracks were healed. It moisturizes my body perfectly too. It is slightly expensive and will not work for the face.

It did not break me out though. My daughter loves to use it on her very dry skin and loves it. Therefore, I am sure all the dry skin beauties will love it.

LIKES -
  • Awesome moisturizing power
  • Did not break me out
  • Will surely suit dry and very dry skin types
  • Heals the skin too
  • Makes skin soft and supple
DISLIKES -
  • Won’t work for oily skinned girls.
  • Makes my face sticky and oily
  • Pricey
Will I recommend Cetaphil Moisturizing lotion- Yes but  only to dry skinned girls. Will I repurchase - No because I am sure, I will find better options for the price. Rating - 3.5/5 Have you tried Cetaphil Moisturizing Lotion ?
