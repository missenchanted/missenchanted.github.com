After going all banana's I had to step on Mango, and I can bet if you are a mango lover then the thought of picking up this soap must have crossed your minds so many times, so here is a review for all you mango lovers.

Price: Rs.250

ABOUT MANGO SOAP: You just can’t enjoy a mango without getting a little messy, so why not take a slice of delicious heaven with you into the bath? We make this soap once a year, only with fresh juicy mangoes. Mango butter, luxuriously softens & protects skin without giving a greasy feel & absorbs easily into the skin.

Great for wrinkles & other skin problems like suntan, stretchmarks. Mango leaves are Suitable for dry to normal skin types, specially for acne & blemish prone older skins.
Contains : Fresh mangoes, mango leaves, mango butter,  Vitamin E, glycerin, aqua, saponified oils of olive, castor, coconut, palm & palm kernel.
My Experience: This is a beautiful soap, and looks like all three stages of a mango's life from green to yellow to orange. Has a very faint mango smell and you have to really bring it to nose and take a deep sniff. Lathers good and is more on the creamier side. Does not dry out the skin and doesn't melt like crazy.
What I like:
  1. Creamy lather and feels very moisturizing,
  2. Beautiful soap, the look says it all,
  3. Very mild Mango smell, almost like the "Mango bite toffee"
What I did not like:
  1. If you are expecting a mango blast, then this one is not for you
This is a great soap to enjoy the spirit of Summer and Mango's, its creamy, its moisturizing and very mild fragrance. Totally recommended, but do not expect to smell and feel like Fruity and Maaza :P Do you love Mangoes ?
