Mud A Lot named sound quite interesting and different and I had to pick this one up, Price: Rs.250 for 150gm
ABOUT MUD SOAP

Imagine a pond of cool comforting wet mud in the peak of summer. Thai white mud offers cooling, healing & protecting natural body & face treatment. This mud, which effervesces in water is combined with cool vanilla to help draw out impurities, heals wounds & clears rashes, which are so common in the summers. Rub the mud side of the soap & keep on skin for a few seconds then wash off using the white side.

Mud Soap is suitable for all skin types, specially for use in hot summer. Ingredients: Vanilla absolute, crushed vanilla pods, white thai mud, Vitamin E, glycerin, aqua, saponified oils of olive, coconut, castor, palm & palm kernel.  
My experience: Nothing actually stood out when I used this particular soap. This is supposed to heal skin, and cool. I found it moisturizing though and did not have to rush to my moisturizer immediately. Very mild smell nothing noticeable. What I liked:
  1. Its moisturizing,
  2. Does not melt like crazy,
  3. Last me around 3 weeks,
What I did not like:
  1. Did not find anything healing or calming about it.
I found it an average soap, and was fun to try a new soap. Lathers well, is moisturising and lasts long. Maybe one needs to use for a very longtime, such effect do take time.
