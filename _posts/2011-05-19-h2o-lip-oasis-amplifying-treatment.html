I bought this Lip treatment last December and since then it has become a regular part of my skin care routine.

Cost : 750 for 8ml

I had bought this product out of whim, while looking out for  lip balm and it turned out to be one of the best buys ever :)

Product boasts of : A conditional lip plumping treatment that amplifies volume and fullness, redefines the lip counter and enhances the natural lip color. Patented marine collagen micro-spheres penetrate the lips instantly and attract the body’s natural moisture to immediately plump and volumize the lip contour. Hydrating and anti-aging marine algae combine with vitamins to replenish moisture and suppleness

How to use :  Apply to lips using applicator. Allow to absorb before applying lip color. Can be used alone or under lip color.

Content : Not mentioned on tube

The treatment has a clear consistency, and can be applied directly to lips to act as gloss, since it has a bit sticky consistency it does not go off during the course of the day, drinking coffee :D and water. Has a very very mild fragrance. As it said so it worked, as any other person my lips had gone through lip biting, sun exposure. I could see the difference from day 1, used it at night and could feel the difference in morning. I use it twice a day and pitch in lips balms during the day.

What I like:

  1. Improved lip contour.
  2. Lightened lip color.
  3. Leaved behind soft and supple lips.
  4. No need to use lip balms if using this :)
  5. Works as a clear gloss.
  6. One tube lasts long.

What I did not like:

  1. Pricey, way too much.
  2. Not so easily available.
  3. Cannot be used as gloss over lips, but that surely it doesn't mean also
  4. Has to be used on a regular basis, or say alternate day else lip lose the plump and contour.

Verdict: One of the best buys ever, has definitely improved my lip contour, color and softness.

Will buy again: Hell Ya :) its a part of my regime.
