The Body Shop Satsuma Body Polish

I visited The Body Shop Outlets last week, hoping I could pick up some winter limited editions, but everything was over, in almost every outlet out here in Bangalore. So I returned back with some of my all time favorites, few body polishes, shower gels and soaps...which means you might find The Body Shop reviews very frequently on the blog. Although I have used The Body Shop Satsuma Body polish earlier too, but could never cover this on the blog... so this post is dedicated to my favorite range(Satsuma) from The Body Shop

Price: Rs. 445 for 200ml

Claims:

  • Reveal softer, smoother skin with a gel-based, foaming scrub loaded with exfoliating crushed walnut shells and loofah particles.
  • Orange essential oil is known to uplift, refresh and energize the senses and provides a naturally sweet citrus fragrance.
  • Satsuma juice is rich in vitamin C, a powerful antioxidant that helps to protect and nourish skin, and provides a naturally zesty scent and orange color.
  • Community Trade organic honey moisturizes and conditions skin.

I totally love The Body Shop Satsuma Body Polish cum mild scrub. I have used this earlier also(this is my 3rd purchase), and had fallen in love with the citrus smell that lingers back on my skin and in the washroom too, making it smell like an orange garden. This is a mild scrub and I use it like a shower gel/cream with loofah. It lathers really well and leaves back squeaky clean and fragrant skin. This does not dry the skin, but you still need a good body butter after bath.

One tube with daily usage will last 2-2.5 months, so its quite economical too.

You should definitely give The Body Shop Satsuma Body Polish a try if you like fresh and citrus fragrance, I mean natural orange fragrance and something that lingers back on your skin for hours.

If you are looking out just for exfoliation and need a really good body scrub than give this a skip.

The Body Shop Satsuma Body polish, is on my permanent skin care list and I am going to buy this forever.
