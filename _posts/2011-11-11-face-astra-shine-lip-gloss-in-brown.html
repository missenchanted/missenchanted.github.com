But sometime back I picked up Faces Astra shine in brown. Its true to its name and does make my face shine... you will get to know that in few line :) Price : Rs.239 for 4.5ml after the discount I picked it up from Fashionandyou, but its very easily available on Urbantouch and cosmetix.in. What I liked :
  1. Its not at all sticky and the texture is lovely,
  2. Because of shine component it does make lips more fuller,
  3. Easily available brand in India
What I did not like:
  1. As I earlier quoted about FACE shine... The glitter in the gloss spreads on my face with time and makes me look like a kid form fashion dress competition. Face shines like anything, and I am left with no option but to wash my face.Siggghhhhh
  2. No Coverage as this color is very sheer,
  3. Absolutely non lasting maybe max 1 hour,
Its a lovely shade, but with almost no staying power and glitter fall out all on my face, I am not going to purchase this again. Have you tried astra shine yet ?
