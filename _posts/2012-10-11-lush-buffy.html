"Oho, its not a pet.. its the new back layer from LUSH" Scrubbing always takes a back seat when I am rushing to office, but not anymore.. I have Lush buffy with me and its one of the most effect body scrub and body butter ever. I have never mentioned before on the blog that I suffer from Keratosis pilaris(red bumps on forearms), which can be a real embarrassment sometimes and I mostly refrain from flaunting my arms. Only solution to KP is lots of exfoliation and heavy moisturizer but who has that much time, and little efforts don't count here. I am sure people with KP will relate to me here. This product was made for me, a gift for my dry skin. It was love at first sniff...

Its a superb body scrub and a moisture bomb. After shower I step away from water and use it like a regular soap in circular motion. Initially it might feel a bit gritty, but gradually it smoothens out and is not harsh, but avoid using on thin skin like face, neck etc. then wash of with warm water. I use it daily, without a miss.

Its amazingly moisturizing, and I don't need any kind of moisturizer after this, skin stays soft and moisturized for more than 24 hours even for a dry skin like mine. Its a slayer for dead skin and bumps leaving behind smooth, soft and supple skin for hours. I could see change in the texture of my skin in 3-4 days.

No more KP bumps but the old red marks are still there (Fingers crossed.. they will be gone soon)

It has been almost 2 weeks since I started using this, but I still have more than 3/4th of the product left.

Even though its a bit steeply priced, but totally worth and has found a permanent place in my shelf.. I will totally recommend this to everyone who has dry skin, and specially to those who have red bumps, KP and require a thoroughly pampered and moisturized skin. Oily skin can give this a skip.. So do you think you are going to get your buffy ? *Product was sent for review, but that has not affected my opinion at all.
