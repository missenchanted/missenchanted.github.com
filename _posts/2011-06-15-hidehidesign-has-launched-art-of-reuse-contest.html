Hidesign has launched Art of Reuse Contest

To mark the World Environment Day, Hidesign, in partnership with Femina, has announced the Art of Reuse.The Art of Reuse is an eco-project which gives you all the means to make your first “Green leather creation” and enter it into a step contest on Facebook where you could win Hidesign Gift Vouchers worth Rs.15000/-, Rs. 10000/- and Rs. 5000/- for the top three entries. Here’s a chance to try your hand at exploring the genius in you! Just follow these simple steps below:

1. This contest is open to anyone residing in India. Register on Hidesign’s Facebook page http://www.facebook.com/hidesign on the Art of Reuse tab located on the left below the profile picture. If you are not able to find it, click “more” to get the Art of Reuse tab. Fill in your name, email address and preferred Hidesign store to collect the Art of Reuse kit,and click on submit form. 2. You will receive an email confirming your registration. Wait three working days before you go to your preferred Hidesign store to collect the kit. Please take a copy / Printout of the email when you visit the store to collect the kit. 3. The Art of Reuse kit has simple instructions about how you can use the contents of the kit. Feel free to use those, plus any additional material you might need, to create something practical and beautiful.The main criteria is the scrap leather in the Art of Reuse kit should be the primary material for your product. 4. You have until 20th July to submit your product to the Hidesign store, after which a picture will be taken of your product and uploaded on our Facebook page. This will initiate a voting process where you must encourage your friends and family to vote for your piece. The products with the top ten votes as on 30th July, 11:30 pm will be nominated. Please note that the sooner you submit your product, the more time you get to collect votes! 5. Hidesign and Femina will chose the best three of the top ten nominees and announce the winners on the Facebook page on 1st August 2011. The winners will stand a chance to win Hidesign gift vouchers worth Rs. 15000/-, Rs. 10000/- and Rs. 5000/- respectively. 6. If we really love your design, there is a chance that Hidesign will convert it into a product line for sale in all stores under the Green Collection to be launched later this year! 7. Only 500 kits are been given away for the Art of reuse contest on a first come first serve basis.To reserve your kit, enter now.
