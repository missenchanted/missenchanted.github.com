Fabindia bitter orange facial spray

Bought this product 2 weeks back and have been using it since then. I use facial mist quite often, mine earlier mist was from H2O(Will review later). I am quite happy with this product very fresh, very orangy and soothing fragrance. Price: Rs.150 for 50ml Product contains: Purified water, Polysorbate 20, Glycerine, Phenoxyethanol, Allantoin, Bitter orange essential oil All above products are safe to use and have no side effects(source Wikipedia). My experience: Its small and cute bottle is so handy that I can even carry it in a clutch. My main usage is after Gym, when I feel tired and need some cooling effect to my face. I don't carry my cosmetic pouch to Gym, it's just lot many splashes of water on face and eyes, and a gentle spray of this product. It instantly cools down my face and love the smell it's so real orangy :). Best in times of street shopping, just take it out of your bag, spray and you ready to go. What I like: 1. Fab India is against animal testing 2. Smells real orange and not like any chemical 3. Cute and small bottle easy to carry even to parties in a clutch 4. Cools and rejuvenate instantly 5. Decent pricing 6. I have a highly sensitive skin, spray does no harm to it. What I didn't like: Till now nothing as such :D My Verdict: My H2O Mist days are over and its Fab India now. I liked this product a lot, infact planning to buy some more Fabindia stuff (Will update soon).
