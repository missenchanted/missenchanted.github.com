My experience: This is a beautiful looking bar, like a piece of rainbow with three stripes. Lavender, cocoa butter and Almond can easily be identified just by looking. Smells like Lavender with very mild undertones of citrus, overall the fragrance is nothing powerful though. Almond section is supposed to be exfoliating, but if the soap is kept in moisture they tend to soften up (very obvious). Overall I am quite pleased with soap, though I did not find it very calming but it lathers very well and gives a mild pleasant smell which is not long lasting. Does not dry up the skin, so need to rush for the moisturizer immediately. What I like:
  1. The pleasant lavender+mild citrus smell,
  2. Bar looks really cute and purple,
  3. Does not dry up the skin,
  4. Is exfoliating,

What I did not like:

  1. Keep away from moisture, as almond might soften up and there will be no ex-foliation (Soap does not melt)
  2. I did not find it calming(very difficult for me to access that)
Over all I am quite pleased with the soap, smells good, looks good, lathers well, exfoliates and does not dry up.
