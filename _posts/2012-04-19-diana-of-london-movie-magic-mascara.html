“Mascara with specially designed brush & exclusive formula”

Well defines, curvy and voluminous eye lashes add to the beauty, but only a hand few of us are gifted with some amazing eye lashes for the rest like me mascara is a boon, I expect my Mascara to be clump free, non smudging, lengthening and last through the day

Price: 595 for 8ml (Available online at 30% discounts)

Products claims:
  • Specially designed brush,
  • Gives the right length and perfect definition
  • Exclusive formula
  • Volumizes instantly for thicker beautiful lashes
  • Lanolin – free
  • Strengthens and makes lashes naturally strong

Starting in the middle of the upper eyelash, apply cosmetic mascara in upward parallel strokes through your top lashes from below the upper lashes combing them upwards, and working your way to the edges. Lightly stroke down the bottom lashes, moving from the inner to the outer corner of the eye & comb lashes from base to tip.

Shades available : Black

Product contains:

Applicator brush: Flexible and handy, designed to give a clump free effect.

My Experience: The packaging is attractive in a bright green colored tube. The brush is handy and flexible. It does define the eye lashes, gives a slight lengthening and curve. What I like the most is if used only 1-2 coats it gives a defined and natural look, and if we increase the coats it does give bit volume and dramatic effect to the eye without any clumps.

What I liked:

  1. Can be used both for Natural/Dramatic look (2-in 1)
  2. Gives lengthening and curve effect
  3. No Clumps
  4. Does not smudge
  5. Gets easily removed, as its not water proof (I prefer non water proof mascara)

What I did not like:

  1. As claimed its not a volume mascara,
  2. Not available easily in retail stores but available on every online shop,

Without Mascara

With Mascara

I would grade this as a everyday regular mascara, which defines and gives a natural look. But if you are looking for volume and drama in eyes, this one is not for you. I would totally recommend this, since they are available at amazing discounts online.
