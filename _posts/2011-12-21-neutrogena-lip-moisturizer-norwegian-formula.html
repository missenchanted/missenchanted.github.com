Contains: Active Ingredients: Octinoxate 7.5%, Oxybenzone 4%. Inactive Ingredients: Castor Seed Oil (Ricinus Communis), Corn Oil (Zea Mays), Ozokerite, Petrolatum, Beeswax (Apis Mellifera), Ethylhexyl Palmitate, Paraffin, Stearyl Alcohol, Carnauba (Copernicia Cerifera) Wax (Copernicia Cerifera), BHT What I like:
  1. No doubt that its a great balm for dry and chapped lips, Its a one night wonder, an overnight application will give relief to major dry and chapped lips,
  2. Taste and odorless,
  3. I prefer tube packaging over tub packing any day,
  4. SPF 15 inclusion is a :Approve:
  5. PABA free, so its good for sensitive skin.
What I did not like:
  1. Although its good for overnight application, but during day time it does not last long, I apply it every 2 hours during winters.
Its a simple lip balm and fulfills everything that it promises. If you look forward to color and fragrance, then its not for you :( else do give it a try. I like it and will buy it again n again, in fact I just peeled a sticker to find that its actually imported from Korea :D he he
