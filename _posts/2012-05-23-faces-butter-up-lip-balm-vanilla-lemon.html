I love lip balms and hoard them like crazy, there is a lip balm for my every bag and room. When I saw the Faces lip balm online, I instantly picked up the two for their slight different fragrances. But I am not at all impressed with them, read on...

Prices: Rs. 99 for 4.5gm Faces Claims & Ingredients: My experinece: The biggest turnoff has to be the smell, specially the lemon one. It smells like the lemon hand soap we usually encounter. Yet I tried it on a few times, I made me feel nauseated. On application it gives an oily feel and not creamy, which does take sometime to get absorbed. As a lip balm it does its job well and does moisturize the lips.

What I liked:

  1. It does moisturize the lips,

What I did not like:

  1. The fragrance, it made me feel sick,
  2. It does take some time to absorb, so you have to wait before applying any color

I am not picking up them for sure. What I actually did with them is melted them together in a tub and use it a cuticle cream for my feets at night. Almost all balm that dont fork for me end up being used liked that.
