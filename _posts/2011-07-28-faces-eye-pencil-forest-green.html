Green, Blue and purple are my all time favorites. Price: Rs. 249, but It cost me Rs.199 in sale Product claims: Soft gentle pencils in a rainbow of shades. Use them to enhance your natural eye color for dramatic effect or give you that bright-eyed glow. Gentle and safe for sensitive eyes. What I like about the product:
  1. Great pigmentation... absolutely love it,
  2. Very soft and creamy, just glides over the eye,
  3. Available in 33 shades wow :)
  4. Minimal smudging, and respectable staying power
  5. Quite decently priced
What I don't like about the product:
  1. Pencil is very soft and breaks if not sharpened carefully :(, as you can see I broke the tip.
  2. I tried blending it with a brush, but it did not work... :(
  3. Not that easily available
  4. Doesn't stay on waterline, if used smudges badly :(
  5. For fine lines, eye brush is needed.
