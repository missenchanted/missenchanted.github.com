Etude House Missing U I can fly Lip Balms

Missing U is a campaign by Etude House to help protect the endangered species and it has been very successful so far. Part of the profits go to animal care organizations. Season 3 I can Fly! is about Eagle owl, Kakapo, Grass owl and Celurean Warbler. 
I have always loved EOS lip balms for their eccentric packaging and shape, but they aren't available in India. So when I saw the Etude House Missing U I can fly Lip Balms on the Etude House website, I just grabbed them. A friend of mine traveling from Korea brought these goodies for me . My Etude House Haul.... No doubt that these look like bird eggs and yet look so cute...

Price: 5$ (Rs. 250 approximately) I have the Bluebird(Blue packaging) & Eastern Grass owl(Yellow one)

This balm reminds me of a Korean drink "Peach Water", which is plain water with strong hints of peach. And both the balms remind me of sweet fragrance of peach. The packaging and shape are cute and effective, one swipe is enough to cover both the lips at the same time. Now the sad part, as lip balm its really not that effective and results are very short lived. I find myself applying it every 30-45 minutes.

Eastern Grass Owl

 Bluebird

The balm is cute, smells nice and fruity. But falls badly when it comes to moisturization. Since I fell for the packaging, I am defiantly picking up the EOS balm and skipping this one in future...

Since the balm is made for a cause, so picking up one piece won't hurt na ?
