The Promise by Nikita Singh

Some promises are worth keeping..... but why some get broken ?
After a very long time I picked up a romantic story from an Indian author and I am impressed. This has to be my fastest read ever, I could finish this book in three hours at a stretch and found myself crying sobbing for the last one hour. The Promise by Nikita Singh is a very fast read, with no boring or lull portions in between. Seems the writer was in fifth gear when penning down this story. Story revolves Shambhavi who find herself attracted to a rich and handsome man, who himself has a secret crush on her. But one event changes the course of their relationship and specially her life forever... Will The Promises be kept.... ??? I loved the way each chapter started with a quote, I liked reading them... If you are looking for a no fuss and a fast romantic read, do pick this up. This book does bring out some hidden emotion buried deep down our hearts and its not bad to cry once in a while.... right ??? Please suggest some new books, I want to pick up a huge lot !!!! Happy Monday !!!!
