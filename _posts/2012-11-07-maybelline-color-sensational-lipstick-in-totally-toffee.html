I just love the neutral shades when it comes to lip colors, as I can easily keep on hoarding them without any guilt since I know I shall be able to use them completely. Maybelline Color Sensational in Totally Toffee is the perfect MLBB(My Lips but better) shade for fair skin.A gift for those who have pigmented lips and are in search for a prefect everyday nude shade.

Price: Rs.299

Unlike my Colorbar Matte in Bare(Another favorite), this does not have much pink undertone and is a natural nude in all sense. Fades evenly and does not leave and tint. I can detect some cocunut like smell, although very mild. Formula is creamy and non drying and lasts me good 2-3 hours without meals.
