Most of my friends might already be knowing about my love for Avon glimmerstick eyeliners, these are one of the most creamiest and budget friendly eye liners out there. I got the nocturnal shade from the recently launched Urban Jungle by Avon. If you love prunella from Colorbar, you will love this one too.

Price: Rs. 259 (Current offer Rs. 179) Avon claims: Creamy formula glides on smoothly for precision lining that won’t drag or smudge, and wears for up to 12 hours. Retractable pencil means no need for sharpening and waterproof.  

My experience: Glimmerstick eyeliners are very creamy and pigmented. Being retractable I don't have to worry about sharpening and the mess. The shade itself is very neutral, once swipe will show you dark shade with a hint of purple, but if you build on the color a beautiful purple will emerge out. Its 100% waterproof and smudge free. Stays easily for 6 hours then starts to fade a little.

I would advice to be a bit careful with the exposed tip, specially during summers as it becomes soft n might break.     What I like:
  1. Love the shade,
  2. Waterproof, so no double thoughts before that splash,
  3. Totally smudgeproof,
  4. Very creamy and glides over the lids,
  5. Retractable pencil, needs no sharpening,
  6. Lasts me 6-7 hours, and then starts to fade slowly,
  7. There is a color strip at the end of the pencil, makes it easy to identify in my pencil mug,
  What I did not like:
  1. Be careful, with the exposed length, it can break,
I really can't think of anything bad about these pencils, these are just perfect. I am in love with these and their variants diamond eyeliners. Will totally recommend to pick these in your favorite shades and I am sure you would love them.
