Winter is all about warmth coziness, comfortable blankets and my mug of tea. I am not much of a coffee person, It has to be all about tea for me. Winter also brings to my mind vanilla fragrance, so Oriflame Tender Care Vanilla has to be the perfect companion at this time of the year for me. But this one is a Limited Edition from Oriflame...

About: All of the benefits of legendary Tender Care, with a delicious vanilla fragrance. A softening and protecting formula for lips, face, cuticles or any area that is dry and rough.

Price: Rs. 199 for 15 ml.

Loved the quotation on the inner side of carton...

Tender care protecting balm is a cult favorite from Oriflame and probably the best selling product across countries. I had picked up the Limited edition chocolate variant and was quite pleased with it, so I decided to give this one a try too

It smells wax and slight hint of vanilla, which is neither irritating, nor very pleasant. It similar to its other variants reviewed here.

  • Oriflame Tender Care Protecting Balm(Original)
  • Oriflame Tender Care Chocolate Protecting Balm(Limited Edition)

Of all 3 variants the chocolate one is my favorite and Oriflame should give it a thought to make it a permanent collection.

I am sure you have also tried the Oriflame Tender care balm.. Did you like it ?
