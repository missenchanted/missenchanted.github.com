Figs & Rouge Aloe Vera and Mint Balm Review

100% Natural, 100% Pure formulations are in all Figs & Rouge Multi-tasking balms
Everyone of us who had subscribed for the February Vellvette Box, had received Figs & Rouge Aloe vera and Mint Balm full sized product. Its a nice multipurpose balm,

Price: Rs. 695 for 17ml Claims: Certified organic lip, face, hand and body balm. Soothing and protecting, Figs & Rouge 100% Organic Aloe & Mint Balm is fantastic for dry, sore and irritated lips or skin. It's intense and delicious! Figs & Rouge 100% Organic Aloe & Mint Balm is an excellent travel balm - keep it in your handbag for all moisture needs.

Lip Balm comes in a very cute and sleek tin packaging in mint color. All ingredients and information mentioned on the back of the tin. The texture is very granular almost like semi frozen Ghee. Its very soft, comes out very easily, and does not require any spatula or nails to take out. A very small amount is enough for one application. The granules just melt exactly like Ghee and give a minty tingling sensation on the lips. If you like minty smell and taste then you are going to adore this.

I do have a complain that balm effect does not last long on the lips and requires very frequent application. But it works as a great balm for my dry cuticles and I am quite satisfied with the product for the same.

What I like:
  1. Moisturizes my dry cuticles and repairs them,
  2. Cute and sleek packaging in mint green,
  3. The mint fragrance
  4. The mint tingling sensation on the lips,
  5. 100% Organic
What I did not like:
  1. As lip balm it needs very frequent application,
  2. Availability in India will be an issue
