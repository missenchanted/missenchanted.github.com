TRESemme' Climate Protection Shampoo & conditioner

I had come across Tresemme during my stay in South Korea. I even used some particular shampoo from them, which I don't remember now. I was really happy when I heard Tresemme was launching in India, and grabbed up the smallest portions possible to give them a test run. Price: Rs.64 for 100ml Claims: Come hail or high water, you can have a gorgeous style every day! Don't let wind, static or humidity destroy your style. Climate Control collection has with Keratin, Olive Oil, and UV Blockers to creates a barrier between your hair and the elements. You can’t control the weather, but you can control your style! I have always experimented my hair with volumizing range from almost every brand, but I could not find anything volumizing from Tresemme India so I opted for climate protection. I am really impressed with the quality and pricing of the product. With one wash I could feel that my hair felt smoother and softer. I was't expecting any volume, since it is supposed to control frizz, but amazingly it did not make my hair limp. I totally recommend this to gals with dry hair, or unmanageable locks. I am sure you will love it too. I am eyeing their styling products now, Any Suggestions ?
