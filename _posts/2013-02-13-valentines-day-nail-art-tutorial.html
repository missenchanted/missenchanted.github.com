Valentine's Day Nail Art Tutorial

Love is in the air and so I thought why not do a very easy Valentine's Day Nail Art Tutorial! The nail art you are going to see will be AWESOMELY BEAUTIFUL and the steps are very easy. I have written all notes, warnings etc. on the pictures itself.

Well, I know my nails are short and the outline did not come out EXTREMELY PERFECT, but the job of a nail art tutorial is to inspire you to try out that nail art. I really hope you will like my work and do post pictures of your Band aid nail art on Miss Enchanted Facebook page.

Also, let me tell you that the fake nails I have shown are just to demonstrate the process to you. I have tried many times but the process does not work well with fake nails. In addition, I have learnt that the nail polish should be or perfect consistency, else everything will get messy. I have done it with a runny nail paint but remember you should always use a good quality and thick nail paint. This is all I learnt during the process.

Also this is my first ever nail art and I hope you’ll like it. :) So, Let’s begin our band Aid Nail Art tutorial for Valentine’s day :D
