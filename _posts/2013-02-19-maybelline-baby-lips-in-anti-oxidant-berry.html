Maybelline Baby Lips in Anti Oxidant Berry Review

I am an almost no makeup person and but I love using lip-balms and lipsticks and lip-glosses and every product related to the lips. I have been using Maybelline Baby lips in Anti oxidant berry for 4 months now and previously I had used Mango Pie from baby lips too( I have finished it, actually ) Price: Rs. 125 My Experience with Maybelline baby Lips in Anti Oxidant Berry Well, I bought it solely for the packaging! I mean how anyone can skip such a gorgeous lip balm. I read the extravagant claims and I thought that it would be another one of those stupid lip balms, which would go into the trashcan. However, I was surprised that it works wonderfully and lives up to its claims. Believe me, after 4 months, my lips did seem a bit pinker and a lot softer and I did not experience much peeling of the lips this winter. It also has SPF 20 for sun protection. It actually is a fabulous product, which I am surely going to repurchase. Packaging is sturdy but the lip balm bullet is not so strong. It will break if you apply too much of pressure. Quantity provided is good and the product is worth every penny. Fragrance is tangy and delicious but sadly, this one is tasteless. The effect lingers on for long but the moisture only lasts for 2 hours max on my slightly dry lips. It has actually made my lips kissable. I love everything about this one. It does not have any color added to it, though. However, tinted versions of Baby lips are available in the market. However, one thing that disappointed me is that the 8-hour moisture claim is not true. Baby lips in anti oxidant berry is the Best lip balm, I have ever tried. Pros-
  • Makes lips kissable and soft
  • Moisturizes perfectly
  • Packaging is gorgeous
  • Worth every penny spent
  • Lives up to its claims
  • Fragrance
  • SPF 20 content for sun-protection
  • Travel friendly
  • Quantity
  • Has actually made my lips pinker
  • Quantity is AWESOME!
Cons-
  • Staying power
Final Verdict- Well, it is a must buy for everyone reading this. It is a product I do not WANT but it is a product, which I NEED. This is all I have to say!
