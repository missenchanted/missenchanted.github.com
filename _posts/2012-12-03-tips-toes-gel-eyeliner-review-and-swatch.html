Maybelline was the first to introduce me to the world of Gel eyeliners and I was hooked to it for years. Gel liners always gave me better control and results when compared to liquid and pencil contemporaries. I am quite impressed with the Tips & Toes Gel eyeliner, it lives up to all its claims and in true sense its smudge free.

Price: Rs 225 for 2 gm.

I liked the packaging better than Maybelline as the brush is incorporated into the eyeliner packaging itself. Displayed in a cute little pot with a friendly brush, is all I want from my eyeliner packaging.

Intensely pigmented and gives the darkest black line in one single swipe. Now the best part which makes this a hero, its completely waterproof and smudge free. I lasts a whole day on me.

I only wish these cute pots come in some more attractive color except balck too.

I would definitely buy these if they come in other shades... this one cute pot is going to last me a long right now. Definitely recommend this to everyone and since you are going to visit the shop.. do check out some Nail Paints too :)
