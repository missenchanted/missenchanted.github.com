Hmmm perfect way to start a beautiful weekend. I grabbed up Oriflame Nature's secret Wheat and Coconut hot oil last month. Its a one time application and was enough for me to make up a mind about future purchase. Price: Rs. 75 for 15ml Claims:Â Nourishing treatment to soften and add shine to dry damaged hair. Place unopened tube in hot water for 1 minute, break open, massage into wet hair, leave in for 1 minute and rinse as usual. My experience: Packagning and way of application is fancy enough, keep the tube in warm water. Twist the cap and voila clear but viscous liquid can be poured in a bowl for better application. Don't remember the fragrance but it felt quite like coconut cookie. As the temperature falls the viscosity increases and the oil becomes more like gel. I did not enjoy my massage with this, it got cold very quickly and felt more like styling gel. I left it on for few minutes(1 min felt less) and then rinsed with slight warm water, but no difference at all instead it my hair a bit more frizzy. I cannot find any reason why it failed on me, also it felt very cold on the scalp.   I am not doing any pros about this product, it failed terribly on me. I find parachute oil far better but I love the Trichup Oil. Trichup Oil for dandruff worked wonders for my sis and she has almost NILL hair fall... she doesn't want to budge to another brands anymore. Which is your favorite HOT Oil recipe ?
