Etude house dear my blooming lips BE101.

Etude House is one brand, which makes my heart skip a beat. The packaging is just too adorable. They have recently launched the Princess Etoinette collection, I am just drooling over the pictures and want it so badly, that I will have it soon for sure (touchwood). Etude house dear my blooming lips BE101 is my first possession from the brand, and I am quite proud of it.

I got three different shades from this range, and kept the BE101 for myself, and where did the rest two go( RD304 Thrilling Red, PK001 Happy Pink) ? They are waiting for one lucky girl, who will flaunt them this Valentines day. About that more details here Missenchanted’s Valentine Day Etude Lips Giveaway

Price: Rs. 450(approximately)

The packaging has to be the highlight of this lipstick, beautiful pink encasing with a pink glossy bow. The bow is detachable, and can be worn as thumb ring(absolutely won't fit on the fingers). The bullet has this perfect embossing of heart and a crown. What more can a princess want.

I picked up this shade after checking some swatches from net, and assumed it will be a perfect nude. But this is more of pale pink shade. This shade totally washes me out. But I love the texture and pigmentation. Its creamy and glides over very smoothly. Intensely pigmented and will cover even pigmented lips.

Average stay power of 2-3 hours. Though it will survive coffee break, but you have to retouch after meals. Fades away evenly without any tint.

Now the best part its really creamy and won't dry out your lips and you won't need much balm underneath. Does not settle into fine line.

I am sure going to pick up a few more shades, but will investigate all shades available in this range.
