  1. A combination of real strawberry extracts and active moisture balancer ensure the optimal level of moisture for your skin.

Product contains: (Key ingredients) Dimethicone, Sunflower Seed Oil Pros:
  1. Wonderful smell,
  2. Non sticky and non oily,
  3. Leaves behind soft and supple skin,
  4. Great on normal skin
Cons:
  1. Not enough for extremely dry skin,
  2. No SPF , so no UV protection at day time,
  3. I am not a tub fan, so packaging is a no for me.
  4. Contains paraben
Consistency: creamy and non flowing, does not leave behind oil or whiteness. Will I buy again: Although I love the smell, but I prefer SPF for day time and moisturizing creams at night because of oily nature. People with normal skin can give it a try. Whats your favorite cream ?
