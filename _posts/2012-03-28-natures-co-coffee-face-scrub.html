There are always some products, which leave impression at first use and one amongst them has to be  the Nature's Co Coffee Face Scrub. I received this in Blisscovered February box, and I have fallen in love with the smell. It just lifts up my spirits in the morning, makes me feel fresh for a great day ahead.

Price:  Rs.525 for 125ml

Nature's Co says: Exfoliates skin, removing the upper, dead layer of skin for a healthy glow.

Pamper the coffee lover in you with our invigorating Coffee Face Scrub. It is full of anti-microbial and antioxidant properties that sloughs dead skin, boosts circulation and imparts an instant radiant glow. Wet your face and neck, massage using light circular motions. Rinse off and pat skin dry. Can be used daily. Go ahead and get addicted!

Contains: Coffee extract 0.5%, Coffee powder 1%, Coffee grits 3%, Vegetable Glycerin 6.5%, Olive Oil 1%, Cream Base, Aroma and Natural Preservatives Q.S

My Experience: The moment I opened this scrub, I was in heaven. I am a very lazy person and I feel so tired and sleepy to get ready for the office. This scrub actually made me feel fresh and lifted my energy. Since I have oily skin, I avoid anything made out of chocolate or coffee for face, but this one did not break me out, and gave a clean and fresh feel.

What I like:

  1. Smell... the coffee, smells so fresh, a slight hint of chocolate too,
  2. Did not break me out, and did not dry the skin,
  3. Gave a clean and fresh feel
What I did not like:
  1. Price, definitely on a higher side, but that when discounts come in handy... check here
  2. Not that easily available in non metro cities,
