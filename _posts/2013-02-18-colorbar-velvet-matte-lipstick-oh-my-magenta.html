Colorbar velvet matte lipstick Oh My Magenta review & swatches

One of the most popular and flattering shades in the colorbar velvet matte range is this lipstick ‘Oh my magenta’. I am a devotee of such bright and bold shades so this had to be in my vanity. Colorbar velvet matte lipsticks come with a creamy shine, yet completely matte finish. These give the lips a soft and supple appearance while keep them nourished too. I myself own 5 of these beauties and love the color and texture of these lipsticks on me. Price: 250 INR for 4.2g Shade: The color is a bold and hot deep pink which will definitely boost up your dull spirits and day. It is quite a wearable fuchsia pink which is just bright enough to be easily worn and look truly flattering on any skin tone. Texture: Completely matte yet not drying. This shade is heavily pigmented and gives a full color in a single swipe. Lipstick goes on smoothly on the lips. The color does not bleed nor does it settle in lines. Staying power: It stays on for 5 hours on me with heavy meal in between. Though the color fades away after 3 hours or so, still it leaves a pink stain behind which stays on for another 3 hours or so. You would need a proper makeup remover to get the color off completely at the end of the day. Packaging: Simple and effective. The see through base makes shade recognition easy. Pros:
  • Yummy shade
  • Highly pigmented.
  • Matte yet smooth and non drying texture.
  • Stays on for 5 hours easily.
  • Survive full meal in between.
  • Not much transferable.
  • Decently priced
Cons:
  • Leaves a stain behind and one need a proper remover to get it off.
Ratings: 4.5/5 Recommend: Definitely yes. These are the best budget friendly lipsticks available for all the matte lovers out there. If you are looking out for a deep pink lipstick, then I highly recommend this Magenta to you. Which is your favorite shade from Colorbar velvet matte lipstick ?
