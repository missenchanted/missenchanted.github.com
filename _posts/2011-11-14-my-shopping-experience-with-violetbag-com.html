I was planning to color my hair for a long time and wanted to try out the Wella range, and after a round of price comparison found the best deal at violetbag :) plus free shipping what more could I have wanted. I placed the order on 27th and received it on 30th. Thats fast, reliable and I could track my order. I really liked their service. I got a call within an hour of placing the order and the order was confirmed. Got a mail next day that order has been sent with courier details. What I liked:
  1. Their pricing is far lower as compared to some other competitors - Thumbs up
  2. Efficient customer service,
  3. Faster and reliable delivery,
  4. Good range of products to choose from.
Scope of improvement:
  1. I wish they stock up a few more brands like Colorbar, Nyx, Maybelline, Streetwear, as these are very common names in our drawer and we would love to see them up there :)
Have you shopped with them yet * I have made the purchase with my money and no discount coupon was used.
