The Old Man And His God by Mrs. Sudha Murthy

Yesterday I picked up  The Old Man And His God by Mrs. Sudha Murthy. She has brought together small stories and events that have inspired her throughout her journey.

Book is a very light read, but not that light to your mind. Some of the stories will leave you thinking of quite sometime. Its not just a read but something to take back. Its a collection of short stories, each story shall take 15-20 min to finish. So its ideal for people who don't have much reading time. As a blogger I was inspired from The Old Man And His God that I decided to start this blog, to keep myself a record of events that I like talking about. I shall come back again to this post in my old age and remember the fondness with which the post was written. Have you read The Old Man And His God ?
