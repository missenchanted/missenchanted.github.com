 Caviar Nail Art

Products used for caviar nail art- - O.P.I. Kiss me on my Tulip, - MUA’s Nail Constellation - Leo
Caviar Nail Art is beautiful and the small metallic balls give an enticing look of caviar...

Steps:
  1. Paint your nails with a base coat, or a single coat of desired color and let it dry,
  2. Apply a thicker second coat,
  3. Pour the beads over the nail, press slightly,
  4. Once dried top it with a thick layer of top coat to seal the beads
Its very necessary to seal the beads with a top coat, else they shed insanely. In fact I would say if you have kids at home these can be slightly dangerous since you never know what they pick up in their mouth. Be cautious.

O.P.I. Kiss me on my tulips is a beautiful pink, which dries up instantly and MUA Nail Constellation is a very affordable nail bead collection. If you love nail art then there should be no stopping you... go ahead and give it a try. Just be a little careful... these beads shed crazily so wont be able to survive household work.
