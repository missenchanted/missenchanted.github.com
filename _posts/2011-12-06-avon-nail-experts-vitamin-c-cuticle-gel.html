A step towards beautiful and healthy nail is healthy cuticle. For a long time I used just hands creams, then I purchased my first cuticle cream from The Face Shop in Korea and my hand care routine changed forever :)

Price: Rs. 149 for 15g, I got it for Rs.99 as an introductory offer :)

Product claims : Avon NAIL EXPERTS Vitamin C Cuticle Gel instantly repairs dry cuticles, moisturizes the skin around nails and infuses it with nourishing vitamin c. The refreshing cream with rich citrus scent will keep your hands nicely smelling, soft and smooth.

Product contains: Water/Eau Glycerin Dipropylene Glycol Cyclopentasiloxane Dimethicone Sodium Chloride Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Butter) Extract Ascorbyl Palmitate Tocopheryl Acetate Ethylhexyl Palmitate Isopropyl Myristate Petrolatum Peg/Ppg-20/15 Dimethicone Parfum/Fragrance Imidazolidinyl Urea Polyethylene Dimethiconol Ppg-5-Ceteth-20 Sd Alcohol 40-B Retinyl Palmitate Tocopherol Yellow 5 Lake/Ci 19140 Red 30/Ci 73360 Orange 4/Ci 15510

What I liked:

  1. Hygienic packing, unlike tubs and brush applicators,
  2. Smells orangy and has small particles which get absorbed when massaged,
  3. Gets absorbed real fast, and does not leave behind oily feeling,
  4. Easy to carry around,
  5. Damn economical,
  6. Moisturizes cuticles aptly, and gives healthy nails,
What I did not like:
  1. Availability can be a problem, as it can be purchased only through Avon representatives,
  2. Not quite right for dry and damaged cuticles, unlike olive oil,
  3. Loads of chemical :(
Will definitely buy this again, since I can carry it around and leaves behind no mess :), but I usually skip this at night and opt for home made cuticle oil. Which is your fav cuticle oil/balm/gel ?
