Monday is going smooth, since I know tomorrow is a holiday.. and I decide to finish the book about Netaji tomorrow. Weekends come and go and my plans to pamper my hair just get postpones every time, since I am too lazy to do anything. Not that I have beautiful locks, in fact you can assume just the opposite of it. So whenever I see some quick fix for my hair, I just need to pick them up and thus I picked up the Oriflame Hair Mask just for the sole reason it has coconut.

I am a coconut freak... I hoard coconut sweets, piña colada yum yum...

Price: Rs. 249 for 150ml My Experience: The thing that stands out the most is the yummy coconut smell, which I absolutely adore... a big thumbs up for that. But as a mask its not a magic product, it a regular hair mask which does its work quietly, while I enjoy the coconut :P This cannot be used as conditioner,  as its not a 2 minute mask. I use it over my washed hair and leave it for 15 minutes and then just rinse off completely. Leaved my hair soft and does not weigh them down. I had been using the Dove hair mask before this and was quite pleased with it.

I would recommend this product only to those who love coconut smell, else its just the regular hair mask.

Just one wish not only for Oriflame but for everyone(Dove, Loreal etc etc) ... provide us with a pump packing... a tub packaging is seriously not good when you have to dig in and it leaves behind certain drops of water...pretty please :cute face:

Happy Gandhi Jayanti to everyone!!!
