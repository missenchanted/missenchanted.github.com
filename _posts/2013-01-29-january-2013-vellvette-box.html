January 2013 Vellvette Box

Finally I gave in and subscribed for the January 2013 Vellvette box, after the Enchantess disappointment. This box reached me yesterday, so I assume that they basically deliver at the end of the month. I am happy with the box, not ecstatic though.....

I paid Rs. 399 + free shipping for one month's subscription. And here is what came in my box. A mini O.P.I., Nyassa sugar scrub and Clinique clarifying lotion.

I am quite happy and most excited about the O.P.I. I love nail color and this shade falls in my comfort zone, so a big thumbs up for that.

I am though satisfied with the Nyassa Belgian chocolate sugar scrub, as it smells yummy and its something that I try on a regular basis. What has disappointed me the most is Clinique, Its one clarifying lotion which all of us has tried since its easily available.
