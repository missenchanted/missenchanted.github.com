I got the Neutrogena fine fairness deep whitening mask with my Enchantess subscription for December. I had heard some good words about this mask from other bloggers, that how it reduces the tan slightly and gives a temporary glow for 2-3 days.

Price: Rs.125 for 1 sheet

Claim: A whitening mask with exclusive Healthy White Complex containing Essential SoyTM, Vitamin B3, Vitamin C and Portulaca boosts the whitening process and helps reduce the appearance of melanin and uneven pigmentation. SMART-FIT fabric with 14,000 micro-holes comfortably fits the skin and ensures more essence can infuse deeply into skin to deliver an immediate whitening effect.

I liked the packaging, its very travel friendly. Just remove the carton and it can fit in any purse or clutch. Its a fiber cloth face mask, completely drenched in some serum. It fits on the face perfectly and I did not face any problem for the next 15 minutes when I had this on. I just removed the mask and dried my face with a dry napkin.

On my skin, I cannot notice any kind of difference, no lighting or glow at all. I just guess its not made for an oily skin like mine.

I would recommend girls with dry skin to give it a try, as it moisturizes the face and leaves it soft and supple. Do let me know if it works for you.
