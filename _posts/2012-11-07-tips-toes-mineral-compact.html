I recently finished my DOL Compact and was planning to switch back to my old favorite Lakme Radiance, and I received this Tips & Toes Mineral Compact in my courier. As an oily a compact is a permanent item on my list 365 X 24 X 7 except when I am sleeping..

Price: Rs. 350 for 6gm

Surprisingly this shade does suit my skin and does soak the oil for sometime. The packaging is sturdy and comes with good quality sponge(much better than many buffs/sponges in the market), which provides easy application, an inbuilt mirror which makes it a contender for your purse. It did not break me out, neither did it feel heavy on the skin.

Product also claims that we can't use the compact with wet sponge to get more flawless look. But I consider using the compact the way it should be use. Although secretly I did try using it once... not bad I would say. Results are almost same as using it dry.

But but but ... this keep my oily skin matte only for like 2-3 hours and this is where it disappoints me the most. Maybe it will fare better on dry to normal skin. But it does fare much better when I use it over the BB cream or the foundation.

Comes in 4 shades which makes me doubt if that will suite all Indian skin tones.

I wish Tips & Toes pay more detailed attention to their packaging, for most of the times we do judge a book by its cover. The packaging feels cheap for this quality and price, and should learn from Lakme & Maybelline how they package their stuff.

Shade I have here is Sea Coral

Overall its an average compact and it does not keep me oil free, but then presence of a good quality sponge and a mirror definitely makes it a contender for your attention. Dry to Normal skin beauties can give this a try.

Which is your favorite Tips & Toes product ? Mine has to be their nail color.
