"Spiced Vanilla" reminds me of those sleek, Smokey colored vanilla pods which I have been longing to see, but my luck has not been strong enough. This cute looking tin is a total loot, I grabbed it at 50% discount. I have tried almost all flavors from TBS Born Lippy lip balms, and this has been till now the best one I ever tried.

Price: Rs. 340 (For me Rs.170 :P)

TBS says: This non-sticky balm quickly hydrates lips. It is flavoured with vanilla and a hint of spice.

My Experience: I am quite happy with this product. It excels in all the fields a lip balm should. It keeps my lips moisturized for 3-4 hours without reapplication, can you believe that. The only sole problem with this balm is the whitish cast on the lips, which takes 15-20 min to go. I wont consider this a drawback because I love the way it takes time to get absorbed by my lips.

What I like:

  1. The packaging is just adorable, it looks like a muffin :yummy:,
  2. Really effective, keeps my lips moisturized for 3-4 hours,
  3. I love the subtle vanilla-spice smell, nothing overpowering,
What I did not like:
  1. Since it has a white creamy texture, it takes 10-15 min to get absorbed. Therefore if you are in hurry you, should not use this under lip colors :(
  2. I am not sure, but either this is limited edition, or comes around during Christmas only.
I would totally recommend this to everyone, specially because you don't have to reapply balm every few minutes and did I tell you, how adorable the tin can is :P
