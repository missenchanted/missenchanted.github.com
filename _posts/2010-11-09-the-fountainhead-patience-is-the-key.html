Fountainhead had been mentioned in so many conversation since my school days, that I finally purchased the book with the hope of gaining some insight into the life of Howard and his obscure and not so easily understood visions. Critics call it a Romantic drama with a philosophical angle. Thats some great comments to start a book with, but what I found reading churned my mind and eyes to such an extent that an adamant reader like me gave up on the last 100 pages, I was left with no motivation, or curiosity to finish the book.
