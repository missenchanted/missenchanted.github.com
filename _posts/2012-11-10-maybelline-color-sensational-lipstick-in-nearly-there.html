Are we there..??? Maybelline Color Sensational Lipstick Nearly There ....

Price: Rs. 299

Its a good pick which I don't regret because even though its has those minute shimmer particles, I can easily carry this off at night without any gloss. Makes the lips look more fuller and the formulae is non drying as other Maybelline color sensational lip colors.

This shade will suit fair skin tones, else it can easily wash out. Can't make out any smell out of it, which is good. Non drying but I still use slight balm beneath it. Lasts me good 2-3 hours but can't survive the meal sadly. Doesn't settle into fine lines and fades evenly.

The camera could not pick up the actual color, in real its more natural than the bronze look it gives.

Maybelline's Color Sensational Lipsticks have become one of my favorite budget buys, I already have 4 shades from the range and equally love all of them.. no partiality whatsoever.
