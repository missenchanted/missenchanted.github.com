Lets start our Christmas celebrations with the LUSH goodies this season, LUSH Candy Cane soap

This is a limited edition LUSH soap themed around Christmas holidays. The complete soap is in shape of a Candy bar and smells like a mint Candy but looks like a bubblegum candy.

Its pale baby pink but if you are lucky, you might get some red pieces with it, but I wasnt so lucky I suppose.

Its an average LUSH soap, which cleanses well. According to me it should be bought just to celebrate the spirit of holiday, beacuse if I start comparing it with other LUSH contemporaries this one will left far behind.

It isn't moisturizing.. which is my biggest need at this time of the year. Its does not lather much, and I start missing my regular shower gels then. Shouldn't Christmas be all about bubbles, candies, and yup moisturizing too ? but I wont stop you, this one is totally to celebrate the spirit of christmas, so I wont stand between you guys...
