I am a lip balm addict and have tried almost every one of those I could get my hands on. I have been using Nature's Co. Lip Chocolate-Mint lip balm for quite sometime now. First a few words about Nature's co: Corporatized in 1984, Nature's co believes in ‘goodness from goodness'. The products contain the natural ingredients that are against animal cruelty, vegan and of best quality from across the globe. Uses only materials such as Glass, PP, PETG and acrylic which are user friendly, recyclable or biodegradable and hence totally environment friendly. The Nature’s Co product range :
  • Atmospure - Inspired by pure air, soothing and calming.
  • Starrize - Inspired by the sun, protections and nourishment.
  • Foressence - Brimming with colourful, exotic ingredients, strong, captivating fragrances.
  • Earthborne - Of the earth and by the earth, enriching and reviving.
  • Aquaspark - Products, like moss, red algae and seaweed hydrate and replenish while invigorating and refreshing your day.
Price: Rs.225 for 10ml Claims: Get delicious lips with Chocolate and Mint. They make lips moist, softer and more hydrated. Contains: Peppermint oil, Shea butter, Mango butter, Castor oil, Vitamin-E What I like:
  1. Moisturizes aptly and sufficient for dry and chapped lips(Winter is here)
  2. Packaging is cute, classy and stylish. You can't get your eyes off it for the first time.
  3. Although it's texture gives an impression, to be hard, but once you scoop out, it melts on the lips,
  4. Loved the fragrance, chocolate.... yummy :)
  5. All natural ingredients, environment friendly and cruelty free :)
  6. Staying power is average, around 2 hours which is quite ok.
What I did not like:
  1. Although its Chocolate-mint, but mint flavor is very subtle and gets lost in Chocolate,
  2. Availability is an issue, I wish they tie up with so many e-commerce websites out there,
  3. Tub packaging brings the messy task of taking out the balm :(
  I just love the fragrance, it instantly lifts up my spirits, and melts like butter on my lips. Will definitely purchase this again. You can visit them @FB *Product was sent by PR, but that has not affected my review.
