February Vellvette box

Finally my February Vellvette box arrived. Here is the first look of what I got. Packaging was in the usual box, but this time in red ribbon instead of gold, keeping in mind the month of love February.

 A Cute valentine magnet which rests now on my refrigerator,

 Blush, Lip balm and  a Toner from brands I have never heard of.....

1. Figs & Rouge Aloe and Mint Balm (full sized)

2. Pure Pro Blush : Strawberry (full sized)

3. Ofra Revitalizing Toner (sample)

Lip Balm looks nice, smells nice and feels nice.. Overall I am very happy...

But Rs. 695 are you kidding..In international market it retails around  £4

Purely pro blush - strawberry... a pink blush with golden shimmer

This one looks promising and beautifully pigmented

Ofra Revitalizing Toner, again never heard of this brand before... so can't wait to give this a try...
