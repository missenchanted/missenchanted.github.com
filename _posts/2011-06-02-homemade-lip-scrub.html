Homemade lip scrub

Like body and face, lips too need to be scrubbed on a regular basis. Scrubbing gives you that perfect pout for that perfect picture :) All you need is dig into your kitchen cupboard and get these
  1. Sugar cubes(crumbled) or sugar sachet. I don't use normal sugar as its granules are quite big and grinding takes away that coarseness.
  2. Olive oil
  3. Vanilla essence - Optional(To give that soothing fragrance)
  4. Chocolate powder - Optional(Yummy)
  5. Empty jar or tub
Mix all the ingredients together, and store it in a fridge, as there is nothing in it that can get bad.(I usually keep it for a week) How to use: Keep it out of fridge and let it come to room temperature. Apply on the lips and keep it for 1 min, then scrub slowly for 30 sec and wash out and dry with a tissue. Apply a moisturizing lip balm. Now you are ready to put color on your picture perfect pout :) How you use your Homemade lip scrub ?
