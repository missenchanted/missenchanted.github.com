If you are reading this post because you got attracted by the title, then I am better qualified to name breaking news.. specially for Hindi news channels.

Time is not going to tell you anything here. This post captures an end to my hunt for the prefect time piece for my living room.

This new year eve I spent good and relaxing 4 days with my most wonderful friends in Goa. I did not miss a chance to check out the Market around in Panjim, and this antique shop was discovered late night at 2, after clubbing when I saw this shop with light and lock on both at the same time. And I made sure that I come back again.

This rolling clock took my attention and I brought it home with us.

I had intentions of buying the Victorian station themed clock, but gave it a a skip, as I can see them everywhere now.

I am planning to do a family wall with clock on top center, it will take some time to come but will share my ideas soon.
