Jordana is a known brand to us, and almost all of us are no strangers to their blush and eyeliners. I received this cool matte lavender shade in Wiseshe giveaway.

Price: Rs 200 (or 1.99$ + international shipping)

About the Eyeliner: * Highly Pigmented with True Color Payoff * Silky-Smooth Soft Glide Formula * All-Day Wear * Made In USA * Available in 6 Shades

My Experience: For 2$ these have to be a loot, but in India it will be a bit pricey and does not live upto the expectation as many other eyeliners in the same price range. Although this range has some mind boggling colors, but this particular shade did not turn out expected, its very light in shade and one needs to really trace those lines back to get an effective look, which required some tugging and pulling.

What I like
  1. I prefer retractable eyeliner over sharpening ones... no mess ,
  2. This range has some really cool spring colors,
  3. Really affordable, if you get it for 2$,

What I did not like:

  1. This particular shade is not highly pigmented,
  2. As claimed its not at all soft, although hand swatch is ok, but color never transfers on eye lids,
  3. Tugging and pulling can't be avoided here.... bad,

I won't recommend these pencils, there are better eyeliners out there in this price range like Avon, Faces etc. to name a few.
