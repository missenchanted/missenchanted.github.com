Nope I have not gone bananas, but rather I am enjoying them in food and skin care a lot these days. After I went all ga ga about my favorite The Body Shop Banana conditioner some days back, I tried an awesome banana bread recipe() and now with Soulflower Banana Skinshake Soap

Price: Rs.200 for 150gm

ABOUT BANANA SKINSHAKE SOAP:  Did you know that sugar & starch is actually great for your skin  Fresh bananas & milk contain loads of both making this soap highly moisturizing, help prevent wrinkles, help heal & eliminate skin imperfections, for a clearer, softer & even-skin complexion. Oatmeal exfoliates while moisturizing.

Natural skin goodies in Banana skinshake soap are ideal for revitalization & strengthening of skin. Specially use if you have been out in the sun a lot or sunburnt!
Contains : Fresh Bananas, fresh milk, Vitamin E, glycerin, aqua, cocoa butter, saponified oils of olive, coconut, castor, palm & palm kernel.
My Experience: Anyone who has ever tried hands on banana bread, can easily say its a banana soap as the texture says it all. I am convinced it has real banana, its texture says its all, and you can even feel a bit of sliminess . I can only make out oils in the soap fragrance, no banana or coconut hint at all. Its moisturizing and does not melt, but I did not agree with rest claims, neither the oats exfoliation claim.
What I like:
  1. Its moisturizing,
  2. Definitely has natural banana there (Love soulflower for that),
What I did not like:
  1. Did not feel any exfoliating action from oats
It was fun to try this soap, but its quite average. Did not find anything that stands out and makes an exception. Which is your favorite Soulflower soap ? *This is a scheduled post, me on vacation till 1st July. Take Care.
