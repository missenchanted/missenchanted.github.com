Lotus Herbals Tea Tree Face Wash Review

Specially formulated for oily skin, this absolute oil control face wash contains tea tree oil, cinnamon and oak bark extracts which remove excessive oil while regulating the sebum (oil) production in the skin. These special ingredients also decrease acne type lesions, leaving the skin fresh, clean and clearer.
Lotus Herbals Tea Tree Face Wash has been with me since very long time, I had started using it during my college days. Since then I have repurchased it many times and Lotus Herbals Tea Tree Face Wash is one product that never fails me,

Price: Rs. 160 for 120gm

Claims and Contain:

Lotus Herbals Tea Tree Face Wash comes in a transparent tube packaging with complete ingredient list mentioned. Face wash is of gel consistency with very minute suspended scrub particles. It has strong mint and tea tree fragrance which is very refreshing.

My experience: The minute scrub are gentle enough for everyday washing, and do not feel harsh on the skin. Face wash lathers slightly and the Mint and tree tea oil smell wakes up my senses in the morning.

It leaves back squeaky clean skin, which controls oil for  few hours. But unlike others I found this a bit drying on my skin and sometimes gives this stretchy feel. It does help in controlling the acne to an extent.

Lotus Herbals Tea Tree Face Wash is highly recommended only if you are looking for an oil control face wash for your skin. I have already repurchased it many times and will buy again.

Which is your favorite Lotus Herbals product ? Mine has to be the sunscreen.
