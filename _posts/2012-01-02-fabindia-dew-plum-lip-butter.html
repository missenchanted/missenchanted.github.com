I picked this up on a total whim, anyways I don't think much when it comes to lip balms. Price: Rs.75 for 10g Contains: Cocoa butter, lanolin Anhydrous, wheatgeon oil, Cetyl Alcohol, Castor oil, Emulsifying wax, Microcrystilline wax, Sorbitan olivate, coprilic capric tryglyceride, sesame oil, vegetable squalane, bees wax, kokum butter olive wax, tocopheryl acetate, dew plum essence, propyl paraben, butylated hydroxy toluene What I like:
  1. Quite economical for the quantity,
What I did not like:
  1. The consistency is very dense or to say thick. During winters you cant take out a respectable amount using your finger :(
  2. Not enough moisturizing,
  3. Have to reapply very frequently,
  4. It has a weird fragrance.. quite waxy kind and I don't like it :(
  5. Doesn't melt like other lip balm on the lips gives that whitish cast if applied more :(
  6. Contains parabens to add to all the woos
  7. Tub packaging is another negative point
I dont know "what part is dew here and what part is plum". I am totally disappointed from this product. It does nothing for the lips. I used this as cuticle cream for my toes and fingers at night only :) Not gonna buy this again. How about you ?
