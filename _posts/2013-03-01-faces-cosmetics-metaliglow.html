Faces Cosmetics Metaliglow

During my last visit to Faces counter at Phoenix Market city in Bangalore, I happen to get attracted to these cute looking bottles. They reminded me of small bottle in the Chemistry Lab. So I swatched all the four shades available for you, so here is Faces Cosmetics Metaliglow...

Price: Rs. 499 for 15ml About Faces Cosmetics Metaliglow: A product so inimitable it has a dual performance. Due to its quick setting, non-smudging capabilities it is a necessity for all eye shadow wearers. Voted Top Eye Primer by FACES customers, this product extends, intensifies and holds eye shadow, allowing eyes to look runway perfect all day long! Prevents eye shadow from creasing. Apply to eye lids before applying eye shadow or wear alone for a veil of color wherever desired. Metaliglow can also be used alone. Or, mix it with your favorite lotion and use it all over your face and body to highlight, shimmer and create a sun kissed bronzed look for a sexier you! Comes in 4 shades and will suit almost all Indian skin tones. I find it best to use as highlighter, when mixed with lotion or foundation. According to me its a must have product, with so many multi use this ought to fit into every vanity. From eye base to highlighter etc this sounds like a great bet.
