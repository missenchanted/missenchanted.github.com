Being an oily, face packs are an important part of my skin regime. I usually opt for packs that dry and absorb oil from my skin. But after reading some great reviews from my blogging friends I gave this pack a try. Even though its gel based, yet it works good for oily skin and does give a skin lightening effect. In my case this effect is temporary but then I did not use this pack daily :(

Price: Rs. 215 for 50gm (Rs.183 online)

Aroma Magic claims: Helps to oxygenate the skin to keep cells flexible. Removes waste and toxins and boost circulation.The natural botanicals fights free radicals, regenerates cell metabolism, helps hyper pigmentation and lightens the skin.

For normal and all skin types

Ingredients: Extracts of Grape Fruit, Seaweed, Aloe Vera, vitamin A & C & Hydrolysed Protein, & Pure essential oils of Chamomile, Lavender.

How to Use : Apply on face and wipe of after 20 minutes. Alternatively mix 1/2 tsp of pack with 1/4th teaspoon of Enzyme Peel Pack to make a paste and apply onto face . Wipe off after 20 minutes. Use morning & night.

What I liked

  1. Although its not drying, but it works good for even oily skin(Wash the face before use),
  2. Did not break me out,
  3. I could see relaxed and lightened skin, but the effect is temporary,
  4. Will be good for sensitive skin since its gel based,
  5. Gives cooling sensation, just like Aloevera. This would be really great for summers

What I did not like

  1. Tub packaging,

I will surely purchase this again, as its quite good for instant rejuvenation and skin lightening. Just 20 min before an outing and I am done. Just wish the effect last me a few days :sigh:
