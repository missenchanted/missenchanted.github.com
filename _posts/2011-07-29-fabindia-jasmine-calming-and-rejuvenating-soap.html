After My LUSH Karma experience, I thought of moving on to a cheaper alternative and plus I love trying new products :), I lay my hands on Fabindia soaps. I picked up a bunch of them depending on my skin type. I have been facing a lot of dryness, which Lush Karma also could not resolve.My first try is the Fabindia Jasmine soap.

Price: Rs. 65 for 100gm Product claims: Calming and rejuvenating soap made using jasmine oil. Product contains: Soap Base (Sodium Cocoate, Sodium Stearate, Water, Propylene Glycol, Sugar, Sodium Lauryl Ether Sulphate, Sodium Chloride, Disodium EDTA and BHT), Glycerine, Castor Oil, Aloe Vera, Jasmine Absolute. What I like about the product:
  1. Very cute looking, as white as snow,
  2. Very decently priced,
  3. Lasts long, one bar with regular use last me more than a month,
  4. Definitely Jasmine smell, only while using it :(
  5. Does not melt like other handmade soaps
What I did not like about the product:
  1. Felt nothing like calming and Rejuvenating, unlike other fragrance based products,
  2. Smell is lost as soon as you step out of the bath
  3. Had no solution for my dry skin, although it was not meant to moisturize,
  4. Jasmine smell can be overwhelming for sensitive nose
I will not be buying this again, as it did not much for my dry skin, but it was fun to use because of the Jasmine fragrance... Have you tried any Fabindia soap ?
