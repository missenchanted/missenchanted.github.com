Have repurchased this product so many times over the years and still continue to use,

Price: Rs. 80 for 100ml Shelf Life: 37 months Product boasts of : The 100% oil free formulation balances skin moisture and soothes dry areas. Also helps prevent blemishes. Product contains : Water, propylene Glycol, C12-15 Alkyl Benzoate, Cetyl Alcohol, Staryl Alcohol, Cyclomethicone, Stearic Acid, Propylene Glycol Isostearate, Glyceryl Stearate, Salicyclic Acid, PEG-100 Stearate, Sodium Hydroxide, Sodium Isostearoyl Lactylate, Dimethicone, Disodium EDTA, Methylchloroisothiazolinone (and) Methylisothiazolinone, Fragrance. What I like about the product:
  1. Totally non greasy
  2. Gets absorbed very fast and leave the skin soft,
  3. So light that I use it under makeup,
  4. Works as great hand cream, greasy creams leave behind an eww feel when working on laptop.
What I don't like about the product:
  1. No SPF, so sunscreen needed on top of this,
  2. I never had breakout during teenage, but now It doesn't help me in stopping breakouts
  3. Not at all moisturizing for Normal to Dry skin(Leaves behind dry patches for me)
I don't use this product on my face, as I have a sensitive combination skin(prone to breakout), but I still use it during summers under makeup and keep it in my bag as a hand cream.
