I have been eying the Benefit blushes for quite sometime, reason the beautiful woven lace looks. But the economy is playing a total spoil sport and alas I have given up on International shopping for sometime now. This is when I got hold of Diana of London Absolute Stay Compact face powder. The thing that I liked the most was sturdy yet cute packaging, lace finish and soft milled powder

Price: Rs. 725 for 9gm

"LONG LASTING, VELVET TOUCH, GOOD COVERAGE, EASY APPLICATION, EVENS OUT SKIN TONE"

- Velvety touch powder spreads easily providing pleasurable sensation in application. - Good coverage and Long wearing. - Hides imperfections and evens skin tone. Apply the powder on its own for a translucent, very natural look, or over foundation for a more sophisticated result. Take up the powder with the puff and apply by pressing lightly over the face and neck. Repeat application over the delicate zones: the eye contour and the corners of the mouth, for an even finer, more natural-looking finish.  Claims and contains:   Available shades:

 

My Experience: This is a beautiful looking piece, I really did not feel like using it :P because I did not want to spoil its looks but I had to. It's very finely milled compressed face powder and comes in a very sturdy travel friendly packaging with a good quality face puff and a mirror. People like me who always complained about DOL's packaging cant pin point anything on this product.

It gives a nice matte look which stays for 4-5 hours on my oily skin, then touch up required specially on oily zones. I got this in Tender Peach shade.

It has a transparent lid over the powder and separates the puff.

  What I like:
  1. Definitely the looks,
  2. Sturdy and travel friendly packaging, transparent lid separating the powder and puff,
  3. Inclusion of a good quality applicator and a mirror,
  4. Shade for almost every skin tone,
  5. Very finely milled powder and gets absorbed easily,
  6. Gives a matte finish, which lasts 4-5 hours on my oily skin,
What I did not like:
  1. I feel the price is on a bit higher side
Apart from the price, this product is a total hit for me. And will totally recommend to those who are looking for a natural finish powder which is easy to carry around without any mess.
