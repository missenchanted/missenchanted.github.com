What I liked:
  1. Very economical,
  2. Looks cute and transparent packaging helps identify color better
What I did not like:
  1. I picked this up from brochure thinking it to a nude pink, brochure color info is not clear :(
  2. Its a frosty pink with loads of shimmer, since it works like a sheer gloss, all I can see on my lips is shimmer,
  3. No pigmentation coverage, but not sure about other colors
  4. Stay power may be around 2 hours without eating
If you like frosty and shimmer lip colors then so give them a try, they are quite cheap and cute looking. For me lip shimmer is a big no no :(
