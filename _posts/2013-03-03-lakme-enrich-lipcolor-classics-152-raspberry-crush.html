Lakme Enrich Lipcolor Classics 152 Raspberry Crush

Lakme Enrich Lipcolor Classics is one of the oldest known lip color range in India. Lakme lipsticks have been my mother's favorite. There are so many shades in this range that it will not leave any one unsatisfied. Every body’s choice and taste is fulfilled. Price: Rs.225 Package is the standard twist up lipstick tube. The color of the package is same through out with one golden rim in between. The package color and the lipstick color are related in no way. There is no identifying of the color except you open the cap every time you need. Raspberry crush is a bright mauve making it a perfect bridal color. I had bought this during my cousin’s wedding. (Ok, I was not the bride there. But, I wanted to look more or less like a bride) The shade will suit every skin tone. Fair skin will rock like no other match. Raspberry Crush has no shimmers embedded in the shade. The texture is creamy and glides smoothly throughout the lips. It does not settle in the fine lines of the lips. No bleeding too. This lipstick does not dry out my lips. It does not moisturize the lips either. What I like: 
  1. Travel friendly twist tube.
  2. Lipstick glides without clumping on the lips.
  3. Huge shade selection.
  4. Value for money product.
  5. Does not settle in the fine line
  6. Does not dry out the lips.
What I dislike:
  1. There is no way to identify the lipstick shade.
 Rating: 3.5/5
