'-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- I pick up some stuff randomly in my cart just because I need to reach my free shipping limit, or chocolate tag is enough for me to grab it up. In this case it was both. Price: Rs. 142.00 for 60 gm.

I have heard some good words about Puro, but my first impression is not that good :(

The first thing that I noticed about the scrub was medicinal smell,

When it comes to chocolate I expect the smell and feel to be heavenly, but its not here.

Not really effective as a body scrub, the coarse particles are very sparse,

Since I have oily skin, I did not try this on my face.

Totally natural, and safe for the skin (PURO is known for that),

Tub packaging is another turn off, If water goes in, that will spoil the scrub,

I would recommend this only if you are looking for safer ingredients, but smells and packaging are absolute turn off :(
