Hope you guys are having a relaxed Sunday, I just crossed a few items on my list  Phewww.....and it feels so good. Shopping can be such a headache....... seriously specially if its  for appliances.

I started my day with Midnight plum, which is a new shade from Avon nail wear. I picked it up as a substitute for my Purple nail polish, which gave up on me and dried. This is such an intensely pigmented shade and only people of love wearing darker shades should give this a try.

Finally I tested the 10 days claim from Avon, regarding this new range. Honestly it doesn't last that long with all the torture of cleaning and unpacking I have been during around. 2-3 coats of this range alone will last around 4-5 days  and then you can see the edges chipping off, but with a top coat it will definitely last a bit more long.
