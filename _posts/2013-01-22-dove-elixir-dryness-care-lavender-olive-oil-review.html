The word elixir reminded me of the famous Kérastase - Elixir Ultime(relatively quite expensive) and Dove's attractive packaging with decent price tag compelled me to give Dove Elixir Dryness Care Lavender & Olive Oil a try. I have to applaud Dove for putting some thought into the design. Indian market lacks thoughtful design and I know most of you will agree to that. Price: Rs. 185 Claims: These golden drops of Dove Elixir Dryness Care work to help repair signs of dryness, to help nourish every single hair fiber and help protect against hair damage.  The golden drops of elixir breathe life into fragile hair that fall easily, to nourish and protect hair from damage. The potion of Dove Elixir - Dryness Care for luxuriously soft, beautiful tresses. To give you the goodness of overnight oiling* in 30 minutes.   Its a heavy glass bottle with a pump dispenser. Encased are the beautiful flowers which give the packaging a feminine touch. The dispenser cap is though a bit flimsy and almost of no use. The idea of a cap for dispenser is a good one, which controls the amount of oil and also avoids any kind of spill. Many would find the packaging bit heavy, I think Dove should come out with some travel size variations too. The Oil smells like Dove shampoo and conditioner and not Lavender. It feels light and gets readily absorbed. I once left it overnight and once for an hour or so and the results were same. So the 30 minutes claim is all in all quite true. Although I found it quite hard to remove it. My shampoo just did not lather(LUSH jumping Juniper, which is known for its lather and effectiveness). I usually swipe my shampoo bar once but with Dove elixir I have to swipe it like 15-20 times, which is way too much. So I am pretty sure mild shampoos just cant remove this oil. Overall I would rate this as a good oil. It feels very light, gets readily absorbed and does live upto the 30 minutes claim. Only thing I did not like is that its a bit hard to remove. I am still not sure, how effective this will be for frizzy and dry hair, but surely go ahead and give this a try. I am sure once you get this bottle, your first word would be "Oh my precious bottle" Have you tried Dove Elixir yet ?
