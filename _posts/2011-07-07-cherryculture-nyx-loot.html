These are the products that I bought NYX rouge cream blush :

Tea rose and boho chic

Boho chic is a nude pink blush which will give the right amount of hint of pink to the cheeks and tea rose is a warm peachy-nude color . The texture of the cream blushes is the same as that of the MAC cream blushes. It cannot be applied using a brush, like the loreal HIP cream blushes. It has a sticky consistency to it and is quite blend-able with fingers.

NYX round lip gloss:

From top : peach, kiss and ballerina pink

The round lip glosses of NYX has lots of shades to choose from. These lip glosses are not sticky but not very long lasting. They have a weird lemony scent which may not be appealing to many, however I have got used to it. The scent is prominent only when applying but fades off after some time.

NYX mega shine lip gloss:

From top : beige, juicy red and la la

These lip glosses are also not very sticky and has a sweet bubblegum-y smell to it which I love . Beige is no way near a beige shade, its more of a pink toned lip gloss. The juicy red looks scary in the tube, however it quite sheer and gives a beautiful shade of red . La la is a candy pink color.

Nyx jumbo eyeshadow pencil :

hot pink and Iced mocha

These are quite good as a base for eyeshadow or a liner. There is one disadvantage that the cap is too fragile and gets cracked easily. I have 3 more jumbo pencils strawberry milk, horse radish and pots and pans from my previous cherry culture haul. All of them have the same issue.

Single eyeshadow:

Root beer, odyssey , exotic green

All are of shimmer type. They are smooth, silky finish and have less fall outs. Trio eyeshadow:

Golden/Rust/Walnut Bronze, Lake Moss , Sweet Lagoon/Aqua/Ocean

My fav being the lake moss :) NYX flat top brush and smoky small crease brush: The flat top brush is quite smooth and does not shed much. This seems to be a good alternative for MAC 187 and quite does the job . The crease brush can be used to smoke out the lower lash line. These brushes are amazing. Just a close up of flat top brush !! NYX liquid crystal liner:

From top: crystal gun metal and crystal pewter

These are too glittery and looks teenage-ish. It looks like a glittery mess when applied as a liner. So, I use it just as a bindi or decorating my bindi. NYX black label compact pressed powder This gives a smooth flawless finish. The packaging of the compact is quite good NYX lip lacquer pot:

ultraviolet and coffee run

These are gloss in a pot. The colours are too pretty and imparts a nice smear of colour to the lips NYX round lipstics:

Harmonica, NYX and strawberry milk

These lipsticks are quite smooth and creamy. Does not have a smell and does not dry out the lips. Harmonica is a whitish pale pink lipstick and strawberry milk is a baby pink colour. These looks similar in the picture but strawberry milk is slightly more pinkish. Strawberry milk is an exact dupe for MAC Lady Gaga lipstick.

NYX nail polish:

Red glitter and techno
