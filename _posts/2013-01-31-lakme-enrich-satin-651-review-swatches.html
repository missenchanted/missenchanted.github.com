Lakme Enrich Satin 651 review & swatches

I recently purchased the Lakme Enrich Satin Lipstick 651 from a local cosmetic shop and thought of reviewing it on the blog! I did not swatch this lipstick at the store and was surprised to see that the bullet has a yellowish-orange color! However, I love it a lot! Do you want to know why I love it, inspite of the misleading lipstick Bullet? Read on…

Price: Rs. 225 My experience: Well, this Lipstick came with a plastic wrapped around it with the quantity written on it and some contact information mentioned. Can you imagine that no price was written anywhere on the lipstick? I removed the plastic wrap and saw a non-attractive, maroon case, which I did not like much. I opened the cap of the lipstick and sniffed at it. The smell is very awkward and like that of medicines. The smell lingers on for 30 minutes or so.

I saw the shade and freaked out, but as I applied it on my lips, I was surprised. It gave my lips a healthy pink glow as the color turned out to be sheer pink. I quite loved it. I have been using it for quite a few days and I love it a lot now. The lovely color it gives to my lips is the reason why I have fallen in love with it. One thing I hated is that they did not mention the price and ingredients on the lipstick or on the plastic wrap. I also hated the fact that it does not cover pigmentation. It has a staying power of around 3 hours without any meals in between. Packaging is sturdy and the lipstick is travel friendly.

PROS
  • Gives a lovely pink color to lips
  • Packaging is sturdy and travel friendly
  • Affordable
  • Has a shelf life of 3 years
  • No shimmers
CONS
  • No ingredients mentioned
  • Fragrance
  • Packaging is boring
  • Won’t cover pigmentation
  • Does not moisturize the lips much
  • Staying Power
  • No price mentioned
  • Gets transferred easily
Inspite of the cons, I loved the shade of the lipstick. I am ready to bear with the cons. I will surely repurchase this lipstick and I do recommend it to everyone. The product is perfect for office-goers and college going girls! Rating- 3.75/5 Did you like Lakme Enrich Satin 651 shade ?
