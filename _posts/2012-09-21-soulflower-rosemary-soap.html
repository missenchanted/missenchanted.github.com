First thing you notice about the soap, is the beautiful pink color. I love such bright color and who won't ?.. Since I have never come across rosemary, I seriously don't know how that smells. But in my words, it is like mild mint or eucalyptus smell, which I totally like. It has a mild and uniform granular texture which provides a gentle scrubbing action. Not very moisturizing for my dry skin and I need a good moisturizer after this.

As many other Soulflower soaps, this one also doesn't melt like crazy in washroom humidity.

If you are looking for an antiseptic soap, which smells good and looks good :P, go grab this one.
