How is your weekend coming, mine just got better with the sales floating around. I am already bankrupt but so proud of picking up some real good pieces in this sale. If you have read few of my earlier post, I am sure you must have remembered the beautiful and enchanting basket that I received from Parachute with Advanced Body Lotions and some really cute goodies.

I have been using these for the last 2 weeks now, and quite satisfied with the results and specially the price tag. With winters almost gone by I consider this as a bit late post as these could have been your best budget buddies. But better late than never, lets get started with the two variants.

Parachute Advanced Body Lotion Deep Nourish for Extra Dry Skin:  

Price: Rs. 160 for 400ml

This is a variant for skin that need some extra care and moisturizer this winter. It comes in a beautiful lavender pump packaging, which is curvy and looks good.

Lotion is claimed to contain coconut milk as the main ingredient, which nourishes and makes the skin soft and supple. I specially love the fragrance, some people may find it a bit strong when applied, but it fades in few minutes to slight subtle hint of fragrance on your skin.

This lotion lasts 8-9 hours in AC conditions, which is good enough on my record.

I definitely suggest this to everyone looking for a good body lotion under budget and specially if you love the subtle fragrance that lingers on your skin.

Parachute Advanced Body Lotion Soft Touch for Dry Skin:

Price: Rs. 115 for 250ml

This is a lighter lotion for skin, for mild winters and regular use. The packaging is almost same curvy bottle in mint but without a pump.

If you are not a fan of fragrance lotion then this one is definitely for you. It has virtually no smell, instantly gets absorbed and the effect lasts me 5-6 hours under AC conditions.

My Verdict:

My only concern with these moisturizers is the lack of SPF. It becomes very tedious and time consuming to first apply these moisturizers and then move onto sunscreens.

Overall these have to be on the top budget picks of the season. Both the variations serve to both the skin needs of heavy moisture dose and slight little care.
