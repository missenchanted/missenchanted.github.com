I feel its high time I share my experinece :) What I loved:
  1. Good customer service, my last order was COD. Since I was out of India and unreachable of phone, the courier was returned back. But they shipped it back to me without any extra cost,
  2. They sometimes sport brands like soulflower etc, which are not that readily available.
  3. Quality of stuff is good,
  4. Additional 5% discount, if payment is made at the time of checkout,
  5. Cash on delivery option available
What I did not like:
  1. It my opinion that sales are not that drooling of compared with Fashionandyou.com,
  2. Price is a bit on higher side, when compared to other sites,
  3. An extra shipping fee, if Cash on delivery option is selected :(
I will definately shop with them again, sometimes they come out with good bargains and during festive seasons they have free shipping :) Have you shopped with brandmile yet ?
