Price: Rs. 65 for 100gm

Product claims: Exfoliating and moisturising soap with aloe vera, sweet almond oil, oats and honey. Especially recommended for dry skin. Product contains : Soap Base (Sodium Cocoate, Sodium Stearate, Water, Propylene Glycol, Sugar, Sodium Lauryl Ether Sulphate, Sodium Chloride, Disodium EDTA and BHT), Glycerine, Castor Oil, Aloe Vera, Sweet Almond Oil, Oats and Honey. What I like about the product:
  1. Indeed moisturizing,  I don't have to run to cream just after bath.. I take my own time.
  2. Safe contents
  3. Mild fragrance, which I find soothing
  4. Soft and lathers nicely :)
What I don't like about the product:
  1. Its not exfoliating for sure, one side did have the oatmeal, but it went away in two washes :(

Except the exfoliation part, I did not have any issue with this soap, its quite decently priced and sufficiently moisturising. Next in Fabindia series are Lavender & Basil and Spice soaps :) Whats your fav Soap ?
