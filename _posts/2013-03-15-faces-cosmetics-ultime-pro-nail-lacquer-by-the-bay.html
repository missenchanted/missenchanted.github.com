Faces Cosmetics Ultime Pro Nail Lacquer By The Bay Swatches

Canada-based Faces Cosmetics launched its new range The Ultime Pro sometime back in the Indian market. Last year had been a good one for the Indian cosmetic industry, with many new brands being introduced and many new products launched. We all had our hands full. Faces Cosmetics too contributed through The Ultime Pro range, MagentixÂ  Nail Paints, Artificial Nails, Makeup Brushes etc,

Price: Rs. 399 Faces Cosmetics Ultime Pro Nail Lacquer By The Bay is a pretty yet muted Turquoise Green.

I liked the nail paint brush, the bristle length is a bit short but the density helps to cover a nail in a single swipe. The formula is not runny and dries up within seconds, but I still allow it a minute to settle. The formula is non streaky, even if you are clumsy with paints, it will still give you a shiny and smooth coat.

This shade is a very pigmented shade, one swipe will be enough to get the exact shade on your nails, but I have used two coats in the picture. I have not used any base and top coat in the pictures, as I did not feel any requirement. Started noticing slight chipping after 4 days, which is quite ok when compared to some other good nail paints.

Although price might seem a bit on higher side, but the quality is worth it.
