I just came back home from a 800km long road trip in foggy situations, whereas my Enchantess December box was waiting for me back home. I had imagined it so many times in my head. So was it worth so much attention and my money.... read on.

Price: Rs.399 for a box (Contains 4 samples)

The price tag of the box motivated me to subscribe at least once and plus they offered free shipping. Box reached me in 2 days after the payment.

What came in my potli

Price wise it did not pinch my pocket, but I was not excited to see regular samples drugstore brands. When I subscribe to a box, I expect some luxury or exclusive samples which is not the case with Enchantess box. Also nothing is customized as per my requirements, as almost everyone got same products.

My box misses any kind of personalized not or discount coupon and the Spa Magazine as mentioned on the Enchantess website. Maybe is a hurry they forgot to include rest everything except the samples. This box is very cold and has nothing exciting to offer except their beautiful Indian concept packing.

I am not subscribing for the next box, but will keep a check, till they actually provide some good samples.
