My experience: I am in a habit of moisturizing my hand every time I wash my hands and that must be 10 times a day. With a cream loaded and packed with so many chemicals, it was a nightmare. This cream is definitely non greasy takes 2-3 min to get absorbed into skin. What I like: 1. Dirt cheap 2. Non greasy 3. Attractively packed 4. Nail is definitely stronger as claimed 5. Smoother cuticle What I don't like: 1. It's like a chemical bomb 2. Paraben is an ingredient 3. Because of AHA I have to apply sunscreen, why would i do that 4.
