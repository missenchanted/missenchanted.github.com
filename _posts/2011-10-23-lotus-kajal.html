"Never step out, without kohl eyes". Lotus Kajal has been latest addon in my purse. My main reasons behind picking this product were this being 100% natural and Smudge proof, as Lotus claims, Claims, price and ingredients: What I like:
  1. Black intense line in single swipe,
  2. If you go by listed ingredients, its completely safe :)
What I did not like :
  1. Its not really smudge proof, even if I don't rub my eyes, I look like a raccoon within few hours :(
  2. Cap is not that really sturdy, it comes off in my purse and that has broken my Kajal tip a few times when it was new,
Although it is 100% herbal and intense black, but I have discontinued using this product because of the way it smudged . Currently I am using Revlon Kajal :) Have you tried Lotus Kajal yet ?
