Lotus whiteglow skin whitening and brightening gel crème

I love Beyonce and Malaika Arora Khan for their sexy tanned body. But i just can't take tan on me, may be because it doesn't look good on me. It doesn't  compliment my skin tone. And the product i am reviewing today is a purchase of the time when i wanted to get rid of my terrible tan. Its Lotus whiteglow skin whitening and brightening gel crème. I liked the concept of a gel crème, which meant that it won’t be too heavy for summer use.

Price: Rs 295/- for 60 gm. It comes in a beautiful glass tub with a shiny silver screw cap on it. The jar is quite heavy and definitely not travel friendly, but good to grace your dressing table. This cream has a very lightweight consistency. And about the gel part, yes it is somewhat a mixture of a gel and a cream. It is a nice moisturizer for summers. It doesn't make my combination skin oily and doesn't cast a ghastly white layer on my face (that happens with Neutrogena fine fairness cream). It has a very nice fragrance, which I can say is quite citrusy and fresh. The smell lingers on for about a few minutes and then fades away. About the whitening claim, well yes it does whiten you, but I won’t  say  within seven days. I used this cream for around a month and my skin tone was back to normal. Happy me. What more could I ask for. So even though I was in desperation to go back to my normal skin tone, I liked the way how slowly and effectively this cream worked. It may sound a bit illogical but I am quite relieved when such creams don’t show miraculous overnight effect, it kinda gives me a feeling that it is not that harmful for your skin. I discontinued using it after a month, because I think that one shouldn't use whitening cream for long. And I read it somewhere that it’s not good for your skin in the long run. Maybe because of some the chemical that they use.  It didn’t brighten me and I was not even expecting. Brightness is something that comes from within, a good diet, lots of fluid and your skin will surely brighten up.  Pros:
  • A nice light moisturizer for summer.
  • Has SPF25 PA+++
  • Does what it claim, but at its own pace.
  • A tub can last for 2-3 month.
  • Affordable.
Cons:
  • The risky glass tub.
As summer is drawing near, I am sure you all must be looking for a nice moisturizer. Which one is your favorite moisturizer ?
