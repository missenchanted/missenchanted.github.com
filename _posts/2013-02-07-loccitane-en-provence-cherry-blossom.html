L'Occitane En Provence Cherry Blossom (Fleurs de Cerisier)

Cherry blossom in my knowledge is a flower of Japan origin, which blossoms only during the spring time, and it symbolizes peace. After World war, Japanese had brought Cherry blossom to several other countries, and thus it became famous from there after. I had the opportunity to see these amazingly lovely and beautiful flowers during my stay in South Korea. L'Occitane En Provence Cherry Blossom is a limited edition collection and I have seen it only on Indian website. This is a cute looking heart shaped solid perfume. There was something about the word solid perfume, that made me want this so badly. Price: Rs.650 for 10gm Claim: All the delicacy and the softness of Cherry Blossoms in a heart shaped solid perfume, for fragrant touch-ups throughout the day. The fragrance is very mild and very cherry blossom. Its not very mesmerizing or overpowering. It will remind you of being in a flower garden and not exactly a strong flower fragrance. I kind of like the fragrance, but the stay power is absolutely poor just 1-2 hours and you have to apply again. Now that can be real headache when I have perfumes which last me almost half day. I apply these on my wrist, and below ears and to keep the fragrance retouch almost every hour... Why you should buy this
  1. Its Alcohol free
  2. If you like mild feminine fragrance
  3. You love collecting cute, good looking stuff
  4. Travel friendly, can be carried in cabin baggage
Why you should not buy,
  1. If you don't have the patience to apply it every hour,
  2. If you like something strong which makes your presence felt.
The stay power is very poor and I shall not purchase this again for sure, but i don't mind sniffing other variants at a outlet.. maybe I might give others a chance. Have you tired L'Occitane En Provence Cherry Blossom range ?
