I am addicted to eye makeup and love trying out different products and colors without any hesitation. Picked up this beauty some time back and love it.

Price: Rs. 295 for 0.35g

Shelf life: 30 months Usable length: Lakme says: A color intense formula, with beautiful soft-color effects that will not budge even in the evening. This eyeliner glides on smoothly and easily, for a well-defined look that lasts all day..
  • Step 1 Line your eyes on the upper lid from the inner side of the eye to the outer side.
  • Step 2 To soften the look, use your ring finger and smudge gently at the corners.
  • Step 3 Line your lower lid from the outer edge, tapering to the inner corner of the eye. Shades

Black, Jade, Sapphire, Purple, Slate

What I like about the product:

  1. Creamy and glides gently, one stroke is enough to get the color
  2. I love the sapphire shade
  3. Like its name, it lasts long
  4. Very cute packaging
  5. Unlike latest lakme eyeliner it is retractable
  6. Have used it on my waterline without any problem
What I did not like about the product:
  1. Doesn't last on waterline and bleeds
  2. Its creamy and finishes of very fast :(
I will surely buy this again, because of its creamy and apt pigmented nature :)
