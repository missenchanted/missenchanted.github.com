Aquatic life scares me, I remember my first snorkeling experience, even though spectacular but scared the hell out of me. Every fish advancing towards me gave me shivers. Haven't changed much since then, I can still snorkel but in panic mode.

When it comes to Sea weeds, they look really creepy too, but I have eaten them, oh yeah when a vegetarian travels to South Korea thats how you survive. It has a faint pungent smell and almost tasteless.

Coming to LUSH Aqua Marine it reminded me of my LactoCalmine lotion, both by looks and texture, with pieces of Sea weed. Its the most gentle cleanser I have ever used, almost like Cetaphil, removes dirt and does not dry out.

  Price: Rs.585 for 100gm

Claims: Made using the soothing pink extract to reduce redness and inflammation in sensitive skin. Those who have a tendency to react to citrus or strong herbal oils will find salvation in Aqua Marina’s gentle aloe vera and moisturizing nori seaweed. Especially cooling on sun burnt faces, Aqua Marina is perfect for normal to dry skin types.

Your cleanser will remain fresh for up to three months.

Contains:
My Experience: Love this cleanser, when I get back home after a day under Sun, it just cools down and removes the redness. By far the most gentle cleanser I have come across which works on Oily skin as well.
I take a small piece, mix it with cold water and leave on like a mask for 3-4 minutes, and then massage it out like a cleanser. Removes dirt and does not dry the skin. Removes tan to an extent.
It is just not sufficient to remove the makeup, so you have to use a cleanser in that case first.
What I like:
  1. Gentle cleanser, which works for all skin types,
  2. Removes oil, but doesn't dry out,
  3. Cools down just like Calamine lotion,
  4. 3 months shelf life is quite enough,
What I do not like:
  1. Looks gross, yup it does,
I find nothing wrong with the cleanser, even the cost can be justified for 3 months. You just need to make sure if its right for your skin type. Will highly recommend to those who have sensitive skin, fight reddishness, and who want to remove tan. I prefer to use it during summers only, as during winter it tends to dry my skin.
