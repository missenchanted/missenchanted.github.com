Its kind of tricky to get that perfect shade and finish in foundation. As a novice I always wished to get that natural flawless look, without any whitish cast, dry and cakey patches.

This foundation is with me since last two months, but I never got a chance to try it out, since its a darker shade. Luckily Unluckily my regular 10K practices under the sun left me tanned, and this shade looked so much natural then. I am really happy and would love to introduce this,

Price: £15.99 for 25ml About: For medium skin that tends to have medium to deep olive tones. MEDIUM 03 creates a rich, "caramel-y" glow. It camouflages any uneven skin tones and delivers that just-back-from-holiday glow. If your skin tone is like Jennifer Lopez, Jessica Alba or Eva Longoria, choose MEDIUM 03.
  • mymix foundations for MEDIUM skin celebrate the golden, honey tones while colour-correcting to masque any signs of sallowness.
  • lightweight texture leaves skin feeling like you have nothing on…but looking flawless.
  My experience: What I liked the most about it is the long lasting dewing look it gives. The formula is very light and very easy to blend. I use my fingers which are just enough for that uniform coverage. This foundation gives a medium coverage and does not hide certain problem areas. Gives a dewy finish which lasts me 4-5 hours (I have oily skin). What I like:
  1. The dewy finish, thats definitely a plus for me,
  2. Very light texture,
  3. Very easy to blend, no brush required for that uniform coverage,
  4. Lasting power is good,
  5. Lots of shades to choose from,
  6. Does not settle into fine lines, neither looks cakey and fake,
What I did not like:
  1. I prefer a pump packaging, for foundations. More control on the amount that way,
  2. Available only online :(
This is a perfect everyday foundation with medium coverage, will totally recommend to those looking for natural and dewy finish. For complete coverage use of concealer and powder is a must.
