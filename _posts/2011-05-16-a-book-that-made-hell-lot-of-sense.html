Kareena and her size zero figure was much talk of the town, more than the stock market's Bulls and bears. Although "Tashan" also managed a great size zero revenue but Boys were bowled out by the perfect Ms. Kapoor, and every girl had a new year resolution and the rest is history.

Since then every every Indian women is aware of Rujuta Diwekar and hers books. According to me its a must read for every human :) yup boys are counted in too :D. It busts all myths and supports facts to attain that perfect health and looks balance. This book never recommends dieting but surely recommends moderation :), slowly and gradually you feel healthy and sooner you look healthy. So no more calorie counting, giving up on those yummy paranthas and butter :P.Best part of the book is the way this book is compiled, with occasional insights into her own life and funny incidents with her clients keep you hooked.

In Rujuta's word, never say your stomach is upsets, its just that you are trying to kill it :)
