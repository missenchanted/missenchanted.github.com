I get bored easily with the same shade of lipstick and when the full-sized lipsticks don’t get finished easily, I feel irritated. I wanted to try Inglot Lipstick refills and got one too! The name of the product is Inglot Freedom System Lipstick Refill in #30. It would be better if they named their shades rather than numbering them.

Price- Rs. 250 for 1.2 grams

Product information from website- PARABEN FREE FORMULA. NOT TESTED ON ANIMALS.

Shade- It is like a Coral shade with a tint of Hot pink. Looks lovely on the lips. The lipstick refill is very creamy. When applied on the lips, it gives a glossy look. Moisturizes lips nicely too!

One problem is that it gets transferred easily. Another problem is that it contains small shimmers. But I am okay with those minute shimmers. It stays on for about 3-4 hours on my lips during summers and 5 hours or more during winters. It has a tangy, cherrylicious fragrance.  I loved the fragrance of this Lipstick refill!

You need a brush to apply the lipsticks but I am too lazy to use a brush and am really not that patient, especially when I am in a hurry. Therefore, I prefer application with fingers.

Let us look at the Pros and Cons-

Pros-

  • Beautiful coral shade with a tint of hot pink. Very wearable
  • Can be applied with fingers or using lip brush
  • Affordable
  • Creamy and hence moisturizes the lips
  • Stays on for 4.25 hours on an average
  • Gives slightly glossy look to the lips
  • Nicely pigmented
  • Not tested on animals
  • No paraben formula
  • Perfect for daytime and night time wear

Cons-

  • Transferred easily on cups and food
  • Need a palette to store these
  • Very minute shimmers which are less in quantity

Do I recommend and will repurchase?

I am sure each one of you liked the shade. I will surely repurchase and I completely recommend these lipstick refills to everyone.

Final Verdict- If you liked the shade, then go get it for yourself. I am sure you will not be disappointed.

Rating- 4.9/5 (-0.1 for getting transferred)
