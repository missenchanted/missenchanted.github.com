 MUA Mega Volume Mascara Review

Mascara is a staple product in my vanity, I do not step outside without a khol and a mascara since it seems so unnatural. I did  a mini MUA haul sometime back and had picked up MUA Mega Volume Mascara for my love for mascara again...

Price: £3 for (Approximately Rs.240) Packaging is very similar to Maybelline Colossal volume Mascara, but in a pretty pink color. The wand is decently sized and one swipe is enough to cover complete lash length. The handle is also comfortably long,

The formula is pigmented and does not clump or stick the eye lashes. More than creating a volume look it defines the lashes and make them look natural and black. But I could achieve a slight volume after 3-4 coats. Don't expect much of curling action or volume. Its a decent mascara which dries up very quickly, defines lashes, is waterproof and does not harm my eyes. For the price tag this is a very good Mascara and I would definitely recommend it if your are looking for something more defining than volumizing. Which is your favorite Mascara ?
