Forest Essentials Facial Ubtan Narangi Nagkesar Review

A clarifying blend of fresh sun-dried ingredients for skin lightening and brightening. Regular use leaves the complexion soft and glowing with a radiant sheen.
Forest Essentials Narangi and Nagkesar Facial Ubtan is my first purchase from Forest Essentials. I am totally impressed and completely satisfied with my purchase, that I have already prepared a list for my next purchase.

Price: Rs.595 for 50gm Claims:Forest Essentials Narangi and Nagkesar is a powerful exfoliating treatment that stimulates the underlying tissues of the skin. By its revitalizing, refreshing & refining actions, this 'Ubtan' helps in the natural glow of the complexion. It protects the skin from Acne and Blemishes. Its regular usage prevents the occurrence of premature fine lines and firms and tones the skin.

How to use: Take half teaspoon and mix with milk or rose water to make a creamy paste. Use to wash face. I use it just with normal water, leave it on for 10 minutes. I take some water and scrub away the Ubtan.

The Ubtan is finely milled orange peel mixed with lots of herbs. It smells like orange and I find the fragrance very refreshing. Packed in a see through plastic tub with a golden cap. Packaging it light and travel friendly. I have been using it regularly for 3 weeks now, and can see visible difference in my complexion. The Tan is gone, and I can see more evenly toned skin. I have been facing a few breakout since sometime, so I would say it did not fare much on the Acne section, but does lighten the past blemishes or marks.

What I like:
  1. Completely natural and safe
  2. Smells actual orange peel
  3. Visible glowing complexion
  4. Removes tan and gives even toned skin
  5. Control oil for few hours
  6. Also gives a scrubbing action
What I did not like:
  1. Not much effective on the acne claim,
  2. Availability is an issue,
  3. Expensive
Highly recommended for Oily skin, you will love it. Rating - 4.5/5 You can check this at Jabong, I got it from there along with free shipping.
If you use orange peel at home, never dry it under Sun, as it looses all its medicinal value and goes waste.
