My experience: To me this product is a total waste, I would count it as the worst purchase of 2011. I am not sure whom to blame the conditioner or the hard water at my place. The very basic requirement to detangle the locks is not even fulfilled even when its specifically meant for dry hair. I did not expect this from TBS, who have such fabulous skin/hair care products which I myself love.

What I liked:

  1. Does not contain SLS, paraben and silicones.

What I did not like:

  1. Not at all moisturizing,
  2. No shine even for Norma hair,
  3. Neither softens the hair, nor detangles them,
  4. Very pricey when compared with other TBS conditioners,

I am very sure I will be not picking anything from this range. I will go back to my Earlier TBS conditioners I am ok with SLS now :D
