I am not sure, how popular is the Avon Solutions series. I had known about them for quite long, but never picked up anything.

Until now I had never used any night cream for my face since its already quite oily, and I almost wake up like a frying pan if applied anything at night.Â Its a big mistake that most of us who are blessed with oily skin make.

Price: Rs.499 for 50gm

Claim:

Oil can lead to skin breakage and thus leading to blackheads etc. Initially I started using Aloe vera gel and it worked fine for my skin, but it felt very heavy and sticky which can be quite discomforting specially when you are trying to sleep.

I picked up the Avon Solutions complete balance night cream. Its a very light gel, almost without any fragrance. I am quite happy with this, as it moisturizes my face, instantly gets absorbed and does not feel heavy and sticky. It definitely balances oil in my skin, as I can see a slight glow and soft skin in the morning without a pool of oil.

It not meant to be anti ageing or a miracle product.

I restrict the use only for night, as its not a complete cream for day use.

Yes I will definitely pick this up again, in fact I might pick the day variation also. But then I am not sure since I am quite like the Olay cream for my day use.
