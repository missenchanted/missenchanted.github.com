Bought it on my last to visit Fabindia along with fabindia neem tulsi face and body gel scrub. Scrubbing is one of my favorite part of skin care regime, it removes the dull skin and makes it soft and supple.

Price: Rs.225 for 100g Product boasts of: Our body and face gel scrub, especially formulated for daily use is made with natural active ingredients. It gently exfoliates then skin leaving you feeling refreshed and invigorated all day long. Ingredients: Purified water, propylene, glycol, walnut shell powde, Carbopol 940, Aloe vera extract. Phenoxyethanol, Lactic acid, Cinnamon oil, Orange oil, Allantoin Directions for use: Apply a scoop of the scrub to wet skin and massage all over in circular motion, focusing on rough skin areas. Rinse and clean thoroughly. What I love about the product:
  1. Cinnamon and orange oil doesn't make skin too oil,
  2. Scrub is quite gentle and can be used on face daily,
  3. Did not break me out, I have oily and sensitive skin,
  4. Ingredients look natural and mild
What I don't love about the product:
  1. Smell, bit strong for me
  2. Definitely not a body scrub, its too mild and too little for that

I would prefer Fabindia tulsi scrub over this, because of the former's fragrance and ingredients like tulsi and neem :) What Face scrub you use ?
