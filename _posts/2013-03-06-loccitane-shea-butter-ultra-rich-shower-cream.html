L'Occitane Shea Butter Ultra Rich Shower Cream Review

L'Occitane Shea Butter Ultra Rich Shower Cream is a very gentle and mildly fragranced multi purpose shower cream. Its gentle on both skin and hair.
Claims: Foaming cleansing lotion slips on like silk, doubling as a shampoo. Creamy texture and detergent-free formula, with high percentage of 5% of nourishing Shea Butter, natural coconut foaming base and soy proteins, leave skin soft and supple, and hair with high shine. In a squeeze bottle perfect for shower and travel. Price: Rs. 1095 for 250ml

Packaging: I bought the L'Occitane Shea Butter Ultra Rich Shower Cream in a travel friendly size. It comes in a sturdy plastic bottle with a flip cap. Fragrance: It has a very sweet smell with hints of coconut. The fragrance is very pleasing and does not transfer to skin, nor does it linger back in the washroom.

My Experience: I like the texture of the shower cream, its almost like a shampoo, but does not lather much even with a loofah. It is very gentle on skin and leaves soft, supple and moisturized skin. I tried it as shampoo too. It cleans and moisturizes my hair, that I even skipped conditioner. Hair felt smooth and soft after drying too. Will not remove oil from the hair. Overall its a great product, and I liked it better than the The Body shop shea shower cream. L'Occitane Shea Butter Ultra Rich Shower Cream has better fragrance and is more moisturizing.

What I liked:
  1. The fragrance is very sweet with slight coconut hint,
  2. Very mild and gentle on skin
  3. Moisturizing to skin and hair
  4. Can be used as shampoo
What I did not like:
  1. Does not lather much,
  2. Availability and price
Rating: 4.5/5 I will highly recommend this if you are looking for a gentle and moisturizing shower cream.
