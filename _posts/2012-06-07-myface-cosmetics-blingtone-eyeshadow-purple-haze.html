Another purple on the floor, its a beautiful piece of foil finish looks eye shadow from MyFace cosmetics. It stands true to it's name purple haze and bling. Perfect for a quick night out look and looks beautiful alone.

Price: £12.99 BLINGTONE EYESHADOWS -- Create an intense, foil-like finish and intense, icy glamour. Revolutionary formula is packed with crushed pearl and pigment; won’t drop or flake. Plus, each shade is designed to match perfectly with a lil’ bling chrome nail polish for an ultra-modern, matching metallic look.

My Experience: This is indeed a metallic finish shade. You have to build up the color to reach that purple shade. Its hard to get on brush, best way is to swipe and apply using fingers. Glitters do fall out around the eye. It lasts me 10 hours without fading with primer. I tried to blend it with some other shades but it was a bit hard task.

    What I like:
  1. True bling shade, foil and metallic kind finishing,
  2. Cute and sturdy packaging,
  3. Lasting power is amazing, I test it for 10 hours without fading,
What I did not like:
  1. Hard to blend,
  2. Can't get enough on the brush, so I prefer finger application here,
  3. Available only online on MyFace cosmetics website.
