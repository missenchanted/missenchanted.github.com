Tresemme Hair Fall Control Shampoo

I have been using Tresemme Hair Fall Control Shampoo for 5-6 months now and absolutely love it! Let us see why I love it so much! Claims:
  • TRESemme Hair Fall Defense Shampoo is specially formulated for Indian Hair
  • Helps reinforce your hair strength and prevents hair fall due to breakage
  • For longer and stronger hair
  • Provides Advanced Technology for longer and stronger hair
  • Suitable for all hair types
Price: Rs.64 for 100ml and Rs.128 for 225 ml My Experience with Tresemme Hair Fall Control Shampoo: I am in love with this shampoo. It makes hair really silky, soft, and shiny as well! Packaging is really professional and sturdy. It cleans the scalp properly but one turn off is that it dries out the hair and scalp and thus a good oil or conditioner is necessary. I am in love with it. I do not actually have much to say about it because it is a miracle product for me. I just wish the effect lasted longer. The soft and silky feel disappears in 1.5 days and thus you need to use this too often. A major turn off for me is that is contains Sulfates, and Dimethiconol. The product is affordable and the product slightly reduces hair fall due to breakage but does not help much with dandruff. In addition, if you are suffering from hair fall due to other reasons like environmental stress, genetics, etc., it will not help much. Pros:
  • Makes hair really shiny, soft and silky
  • Does control hair fall due to breakage slightly
  • Professional packaging
  • Fragrance is mild
  • Cleans the scalp and hair properly
  • Affordable
  • Available in 2 sizes
Cons:
  • Contains SLS and Dimethiconol
  • Dries out the hair and scalp
  • Not meant for dandruff prone and dry hair
Final verdict I have finished the small bottle and half of the big bottle and I am bored with it. I want to try other variants of Trésemme and shampoos from other brands too! Therefore, I am not going to purchase this one anymore. However, I do recommend this to girls with oily scalp. Rating - 4/5
