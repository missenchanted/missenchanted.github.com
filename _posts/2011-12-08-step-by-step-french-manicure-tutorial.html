After rounds of bright colors and crazy nail arts, I always come back to the simple and elegant French Manicure. Some more pics... 1. Trim nails and clean them for any greasiness and stains (Use Lemon/Vinegar and any whitening toothpaste) 2. Shape and buffer. Buffering is essential to get that smooth and shiny base, 3. Nourish your cuticles and use a nourishing base coat(Avon peeling and brittleness solver) 4.  Apply white tips (Lotus Color Dew White) 5. Apply Nude pink shade(I usually skip this step) and a top coat to make the manicure last long. 6. Twist : Added a glitter line (NYX Nail Art) Do share your tips, how you like your manicure ?
