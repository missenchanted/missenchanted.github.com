We all have something Bling in our wardrobes, specially those clutches and shoes and that evening party dress, but did you forget the nail, I bet you did ......

MyFaceCosmetics have introduced the Blingbar for all Skin tones and I will be reviewing the Bling for nails in this post.

Price: £  5.99 and can be bought from here

Product claims:

Available shades

My Experience: This is a shimmery smooth color in purple, in a cute looking bottle. It looks like molten purple metals, and I was all drooling over it. Purple is my favorite, and I never seem to have enough purple in my closet. The Brush is good quality and gives a streak free finish. This is not very quick drying and does take some time to dry off, before another application. Now comes the best part, a single coat is enough to give a smooth, opaque and desired finish. Like all shimmery shades, this has a long staying power of 3-4 days without chipping, and if used with 2 coats and a top coat, this might last more than a week.

What I liked:

  1. Stands true to its name BLING and gives a dreamy molten metal look,
  2. Has good stay power,
  3. Really good amount of polish for the price,
  4. Streak free application,
  5. Good pigmentation,
What I did not like:
  1. Takes some time to dry off,
  2. Not that easily available in India, can be bought online from here
I would totally recommend this if you love metallics and want to try some really fun colors. Price may seem a bit high, but then the amount of polish that comes along justifies it. I just wish that it was a quick dry color for this price. Have you tried the MyFaceCosmetics products ?
