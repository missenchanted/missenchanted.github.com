After using Lakme insta eye liner for a long time, this was my second eye liner and I was quite happy with it for some time, till I found Lakme's nine to five range :)

Price: Rs. 199 for 3.5ml(I got it for 170 - from discount shop @ Commercial street)

Product boasts of : Enhance your eyes with the blackest of black eyeliner that doesn't fade. The rich, intense color in a silky formula glides on - lining, defining with the greatest of ease. Water resistant and fast drying. It wont budge for hours. The brush handle offers a comfortable grip so you can create dark, dramatic eyes that are a night time glam staple.

Shades available: Black

What I love about the product:

  1. Its Jet black, you don't need to apply 2-3 coats to get that color :)
  2. Its smudge proof unless you rub it with a napkin or something like that,
  3. Cute and handy packaging(Purple and gold)
What I dont like about the product:
  1. SA told me its waterproof, but its not. One wash is sufficient to clean it
  2. Brush is quite thick, a fine line is not easily possible, but that doesn't concern me,
  3. Not much of an improvement over Lakme insta liner, except packaging and steady brush, so I find it pricey.
I wont be buying this again, as I prefer pencil liners over liquid ones now :)
