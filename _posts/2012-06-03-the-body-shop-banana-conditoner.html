This is my favorite conditioner ever, its just so yummy...... no I did not eat it ewww, but I was surely tempted many a times :) Price: Rs.515 for 250 ml About:
A beauty classic returns. This thick and creamy conditioner leaves hair beautifully soft and tangle-free. It contains real banana puree.
  • Conditions
  • Leaves hair soft and tangle-free
  • Thick and creamy
  • Bottle made from 100% recycled plastic
Contains:Water (Solvent/Diluent), Musa Paradisica (Banana) Fruit (Emollient/Lubricant), Cetearyl Alcohol (Emulsifier), Cetrimonium Chloride (Hair Conditioner), Phenoxyethanol (Preservative), Hydroxyethylcellulose (Stabiliser/Thickener), Lecithin (Stabiliser/Emulsifier), Propylene Glycol (Humectant), Panthenol (Skin/Hair Conditioning Agent), Fragrance (Fragrance), Benzyl Alcohol (Preservative), Sodium Hydroxide (pH Adjuster), Ascorbic Acid (Antioxidant), Yellow 5 (Colour), Yellow 6 (Colour).   My experience: I am in love with this conditioner, it smells and feels like banana puree. It gives my hair lots of volume and is not at all greasy. Perfect formula for greasy scalp and limp hair. This is my third bottle in a row. What I like:
  1. The fragrance definitely,
  2. It moisturizes and gives volume,
  3. Silicone, sulphate and paraben free
What I did not like:
  1. The consistency is a bit dense, so its hard to get it when a little is remaining
Me and my sister just love this conditioner. Would totally recommend this if you are looking for volume, or if your conditioners leave you with limp hair. Which is your favorite conditioner ?
