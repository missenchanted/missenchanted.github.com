Isn't she adorable. Ok you can't say no here, because she is adorable. Its a nice coincidence that the day I picked up this cutie, I was gifted this Boots Hello Kitty Liberty Lip Tint in pale pink by one of my very dear friends who was visiting from London and she had picked this up on her way here.

Boots Hello Kitty Liberty Collection is a Limited Edition Christmas collection and is just too cute to describe in words. I would have just picked it up for the sake of tin alone.

 The tin packaging is really adorable and travel friendly. This lip tint also works as a balm, though not moisturizing enough for chapped lips.

 This shade is paler baby pink and does not look natural on my tinted lips. I would suggest these only to people with lighter and non pigmented lips.
