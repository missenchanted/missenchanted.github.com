My Experience: I like this product quite a lot. Specially this shade Raspberry, which is completely matte looking and is almost "My lips but better" shade. The formula is quite creamy and highly pigmented, one swipe is enough, no need to redo or touch up. Leave it for 2-3 minutes to dry up completely and you are done for the day. Its completely non transferable and lasts me till evening easily. This can be easily removed with face wash or other cleansers unlike the metallic shimmer variants. The formula is non drying, but you need to moisturize your lips nicely, else the look might not turn out good.
