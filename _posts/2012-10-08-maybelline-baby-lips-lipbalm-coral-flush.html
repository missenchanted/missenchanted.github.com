Maybelline is all over the blogosphere for their Color Tattoo and baby lips. I am sure by now almost all of us must have got at least one in hand... if not then you should. Although its nothing great when compared to existing Maybelline lip balms.. which I totally adore, Price: Rs.150 for tinted ones and Rs.125 for plain

Claims:
  • Visibly renewed lips and 8-hr hydration
  • The secret: Exclusive lip renew formula with SPF 20
  • Protects and moisturizes lips for a full 8 hours
  • After 4 weeks, lips are visibly renewed and reborn
  • 7 varieties

My Experience: Packaging is not as cute as their foreign counterparts, but I won't cry out for that since.. these babies live up to each and every claim they make. From the first application itself, you can see a change in the way your lips feel moisturized. But this feel remains, till the time the balm is on the lips. and then you have to apply again.

  1. Loved the mild fragrance, which does stay for sometimes and then fades,
  2. Very very moisturizing, you can feel the difference,
  3. Lasts after light snacks and drinks.. woaahhh.. thats super good,
  4. Come with SPF 20
  5. Gives a glossy finish,

I just wish,

  1. These were a little more pigmented as their predecessors,
  2. Please please give us a more cuter packing such as EOS balms..

So overall its a great balm, which definately lasts on the lips longer than many counterparts, but lips feel dry again after sometime say.. 5 hours and then you need to reapply.

Highly recommend to everyone.. no excpetion, but dont expect color atleast from Coral Flush.

Health and glow is giving 10% off these.. last time I checked :)
