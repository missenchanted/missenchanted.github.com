Ponds Daily Face Wash Active Cleansing System Review

I have been using Cetaphil Cleansing lotion for a year now and absolutely love it. Just to take a break, I purchased the Ponds daily face wash active cleansing system. Let us see if it worked for me, Price: Rs. 90 for 100 grams My Experience with Ponds Daily Face Wash Active Cleansing System: I did like the face wash, however this face wash contains SLS and paragons. Therefore, I will not be repurchasing it anymore. It claims that it will not leave any slippery feeling behind but it does and I have to splash my face with water for like a hundred times to get this off. The packaging is nice and sturdy and the product is travel friendly due to the tight flip cap. The texture of the face wash is thick and slimy. A very small amount is needed and it foams perfectly. If you are in a hurry then don’t even think of using this to clean your face because it take a long time to get off the face. Nevertheless, it does not dry out the face much and it has never broken me out. I have been using it for a few weeks and I like it as it cleans the skin properly. I hope Ponds makes this face wash Paraben and SLS free. PROS-
  • Packaging
  • Affordable
  • Does not dry out the skin
  • Cleans properly
  • Did not break me out
  • Very small amount is needed to clean the face
  • Fragrance
CONS-
  • Contains SLS and parabens
  • Leaves a slippery feel behind
Final verdict- I liked the face wash and it is worth a try. However, I will not be repurchasing it because my skin does not like parabens and SLS. Rating- 3.5/5 Whats your opinion about Ponds Daily Face Wash Active Cleansing System ?
