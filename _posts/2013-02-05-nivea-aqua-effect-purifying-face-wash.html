Nivea Aqua Effect Purifying Face Wash

I am a loyalist when it comes to my face wash, I have stuck with my Clean and Clear Deep Action Cleanser for years and it still remains my best face wash ever. It was on my side when I needed it the most, but then I thought I should start experimenting a bit more else I might miss out on some really good products and launches. I saw Nivea Aqua Effect Purifying Face Wash and brought it home with me. I also got the Nivea Pure Effect Total Face Cleanup Wash, but more on that in later reviews

Price: Rs. 99 for 75 ml

Claims:

  • Deeply cleanses pores with purifying peeling scrubs to reduce and prevent impurities.
  • Reduces excess sebum to prevent oily shine with its purifying formula while respecting the skin’s own moisture balance.
  • Vitalizes the skin with Ocean Algae.

Nivea Aqua Effect Purifying Face Wash is of gel consistency, with suspended scrubbing particles. It smells exactly like the old nivea cream in the blue tin(Remember). The scrubs looks harmless, but be cautious as it left me with with burning sensation due to excessive scrubbing.

I agree its a good scrub and face wash for combination to oily skin. Gives me a squeaky clean feel and removes light makeup too. But coming to the oil balancing claim, it does not stand much before my favorite Clean and Clear Deep Action Cleanser(C&C gives me 6-7 hours of matte skin).

I limit my use of Nivea Aqua Effect Purifying Face Wash only for nights.  Sensitive skin beauties and broken skin should definitely stay away.

I would anyday prefer the Neutrogena deep clean blackhead eliminating daily scrub  over this because its not that harsh. Overall for the price its a decent product.
