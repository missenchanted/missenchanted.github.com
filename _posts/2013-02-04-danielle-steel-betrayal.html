Book Review: Danielle Steel - Betrayal

I have always had a good read from Daniel Steel. She has this very unique way of introducing her characters. If you have read her books, I am pretty sure you will understand what that means. This is my third pick from her after Lone Eagle and Sisters. Danielle Steel - Betrayal is a story of a talented Hollywood Director and the betrayal she faces from the people she loved the most.

First 1-4 chapters dedicatedly emphasize on explaining the various characters again and again. But then that's the case with every DS book that I have ever read. You will expect some changes or excitement during the read, but the story is very plain and simple.

Why you should pick this book, because
  • This book has emotions(a lot of sobbing and warmth both),
  • If you are looking for a light read on your vacation.
  • A story which promises optimism and leaves you with a smile on your face and a hope in your heart.
Why you should not read this,
  • If you are looking from some suspense, thrill.
