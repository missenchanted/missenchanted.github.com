I don't believe in whitening creams, I put my faith in is healthy, and beautifully toned skin. I have acne prone skin which does leave me with seldom marks, I am sure you know how that feels :(

I have been using Pond's white beauty for last one month and am quite impressed, it did give noticeable difference in 7 days. Ok nothing like whitening but definitely a better skin texture, slightly faded marks and thats quite a lot for one week.

Price: Rs. 249 for 50gm

My Experience: This has become my HG day cream, gives me matte skin and controls oil. It has given me back this beautiful skin texture which I am in love with. This cream needs to be blended properly and very fast, else it might not get absorbed and you might need slight water/rose water and blend again.

Ponds has changed their packaging... they are heavy on your purse now.... I mean that by weight :P, Seriously the lid is quite heavy and difficult to carry around.

What I like:
  1. Feels very light and gets absorbed within seconds,
  2. Comes with SPF 20,
  3. Definitely evens out the skin tone and slowly fades away mark,
  4. Did not break me out, (I have oily and acne prone skin),
  5. Gives a matte finish, works as good makeup base,
What I did not like:
  1. I believe it will suit only Normal-Combination skin only,
  2. The lid is really heavy to carry in your bag.

I will totally recommend this to beauties with Oily or combination skin, and believe me you will just love it. It helps with even skin tone, fades marks, oil control and sun protection.

Now I am tempted to try out their night cream too...any suggestions? *Product was sent by the company, but that has not affected the review.
