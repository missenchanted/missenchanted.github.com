Oriflame Swedish Spa Night renewal Hand Cream

I am so much into Oriflame products now days that my friends have started teasing me that Oriflame is going to send me a nice and heavy cheque or gift hamper for increasing their turnover drastically. I really wish they do so... today the product I am going to talk about is Oriflame Swedish Spa Night renewal Hand Cream.

This hand cream was a purchase for winters, when my hands were terribly parched and dry. So on my quest to find something good enough for my hand i stumbled into this cream. Price: Rs259 for 75 ml. I got it at a discount for Rs 180. Oriflame Swedish Spa Night renewal Hand Cream is a part of Oriflame's swedish spa range. It comes in a white tube with a sturdy flip flop cap. Nothing fancy but nothing bad either. It has a normal creamy texture and gets absorbed within 10-15 seconds  of application. Although this cream is a night renewal hand cream. but it is not that worthy. First of all, being a night  TREATMENT, I expected it to be thicker in consistency. But the  cream gets absorbed within seconds of application and I feel like I have applied nothing. Well, I still go to sleep hoping that next  day I am going to wake up with supple hands but nothing happens. My hands are just like what they would be if i had applied any regular cream. Swedish spa range from oriflame is raved for its fragrance part, but i quite don’t understand the fuss. True, this cream has a unique smell but it doesn’t seem good or appealing to me. I asked my mom and my husband about it and they both said that it’s weird. Even they were unsure as what kind of a smell is that. After trying many a times i could finally find out...it smells like cinnamon.
Pros-
  1. Not so costly.
  2. Absorbs easily (for me it’s not the best, because it’s a night cream and i expect it to be rich and intense.)
Cons-
  1. I did not like the smell, nothing spa or luxurious about it,
  2. Don't fool yourself with this night renewal thing. It’s just a normal hand cream.
