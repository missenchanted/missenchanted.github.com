L'occitane Cherry Blossom Shower Gel

I had picked up few stuff from L'occitane sometime back in their online sale. So far L'occitane has failed to impress me. I don't deny the fact that they are leader when it comes to fragrance. L'occitane Cherry Blossom Shower Gel is beautiful aromatic clear shower gel......

Price: Rs. 1,095.00 for 250ml (In the pictures its 25ml) Claims: This gel gently cleanses the skin, leaving it delicately perfumed. Used as a foaming bath, it transforms into a rich and smooth foam, releasing the delicate and captivating Cherry Blossom fragrance. Contains a cherry extract from Luberon, France.

Ingrediants :

Loccitane products are known for their natural ingredients and amazing pallet of fragrance. Indeed the shower gel smells cherry blossom. Comes in a see through and sturdy bottle with a flip cap. the packaging makes the shower gel travel friendly. It lathers good and cleans well. It leaves my skin dry, and a moisturizer is needed immediately. Fragrance is short lived and does not transfer onto the skin.

Now the part where L'occitane failed to impress is definitely the price tag. I am crazy for natural and organic products but I would any day buy LUSH or The Body Shop shower gel in cherry blossom since they do not pinch my pocket.
