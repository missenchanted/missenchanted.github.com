Price: INR 650/- for 3.5 gm Claims: More delicious than peaches and cream? We like to think so. Our velvety smooth mineral Rouge Cream Blush offers a sheer wash of radiant color. The glorious payoff is dewy, sultry and long-lasting. Available in 12 shades. My Experience: I just love this shade, it indeed gives the me the most natural flush. There is just nothing wrong about these cream blushes, except the lasting power. I kind of like NYX blushes both powder and cream, they have good pigmentation, some great shades and cost a dime, if bought in US. The packaging is quite sturdy and transparent cover makes it easy to identify(Although I don't have that many blushes) I use fingers to dab the blush on my cheeks and pat them to spread properly, this way I get better control rather then using it with a blush. On my oily cheeks it lasts for 3-4 hours and then fades drastically. *Avoid these during summers or outdoor trip, it might melt in heat :( , but a total thumbs up for the winter season... for sure. I am not much of a blush person, but this one particular shade I totally love. It gives a very authentic natural glow. Will definitely recommend these if you are afraid of colors on your cheek, believe me its the best shade for shy girls like us :) Which is your favorite Blush brand and shade ?
