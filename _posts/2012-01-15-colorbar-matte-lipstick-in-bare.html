After coming across a couple of swatches I knew this is the shade for me. Grabbed it from stylecraze and love this shade a lot. This is more like my lip shade but better :)(a cliche but true by every means) Price: Rs. 249 but I got it online for Rs. 203 My experience: Its a natural brown color with peachy undertones. Its a true nude shade for Indian skin tone, with good pigmentation coverage and decent lasting power. What I like:
  1. I love the color, its a true natural color,
  2. Priced very decently,
  3. Very creamy and just glides over the lips, pigmentation coverage is good,
  4. It lasts me 2-3 hours, but after lunch breaks touch up is a must,
What I did not like:
  1. For this price range its quite a deal, but people who love MAC or similar brand may not love it that much :(
I love this shade, and prefer it over Maybelline "My Mahogany" for it suits my skin tone. I would surely buy some more shades from this range. Have you tried Colorbar matte lipsticks ?
