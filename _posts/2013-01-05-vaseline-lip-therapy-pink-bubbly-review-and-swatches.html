You just cannot ignore the beautiful Pink Bubbly Vaseline Lip therapy container if you ever come across it. I just wish brands take up Indian markets a bit more seriously and bring in some more cute and fun stuff like this, hello kitty etc etc..

I have practically lived in South Korea for a year and miss so many female oriented stuff and packaging, which are almost non existent in India, and this has to be one of those.

Price: £3.49

Claim: Luxury living is all the rage, but this time it’s all about letting your lips enjoy a champagne lifestyle. By popular demand, cult beauty brand Vaseline Lip Therapy has created an exciting and decadent new limited edition flavour, Pink Bubbly. Available exclusively at Selfridges, Vaseline Pink Bubbly has a gorgeous rosé hue. Enriched with champagne grape extract, this luxurious jelly will intensively moisturise and smooth, creating a perfectly kissable pout. Simply glide on over your favourite colour to give lips A-list colour that shimmers, or use over bare lips for a more natural demure look.

My dear friend got me this from London, so thoughtful of her. Although the packaging may say delicately tinted, but its not. Its a clear balm with almost no color. It looks so pretty that I dreaded using it for the first time.

Smell is really nice and fruity nothing like champagne which I find very strong and non pleasing to my nose.I find it quite moisturizing even for winter, but have big doubts that this would be enough for chapped lips too.

Its a mild glossy balm with lovely fragrance and a beautiful packaging with a great price tag.

A good fun purchase which moisturizes lip, provides a glossy look and can be topped over lip color.

Ok enough of my rants.... All these brands out there we need cute and girly stuff in India too... yup we need Hello kitty, the pink and purple tins... also you can hire me for designing.. I swear I won't charge you :)
