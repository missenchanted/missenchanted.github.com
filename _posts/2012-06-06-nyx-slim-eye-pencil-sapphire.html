This has to be my last blue eye pencil, I have hoarded up more blue that I ever use :( But this is definitely not my last NYX pencil for sure,

  Price: Rs.300 of 3$ (International price) NYX Claims:

Classic NYX eye pencil applies smoothly and blends easily in a variety of shades.

My experience: Won't say these are the best eyeliners I have ever used. But its creamy to an extent and requires slight pressure to get that color on. But the best thing about this range has to be the price and the large range of shades. You will get lost in the pool of colors and would love to get hold of each piece, if it was just 3$ for us Indians. Its waterproof, easy to sharpen and lasts 6-7 hours on my oily lids and then starts to fade slowly.

  What I like:  
  1. Last 6-7 hours then start to fade on my oily lids,
  2. They are soft and creamy, so no tugging and pulling,
  3. Affordable if bought in $ from international websites,
  4. Rich pigmentation and amazing colors to choose from,
  5. Soft and creamy
  6. Water proof
What I did not like:
  1. Needs sharpening, i prefer retractable pencils to avoid sharpening mess,
  2. Might smudge a little if you rub the eyes
I am definitely buying a few more, they are totally worth the money and have some really beautiful colors. Which is your favorite NYX slim eye pencil ?
