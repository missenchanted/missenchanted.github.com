Have you dreamt about soaking in cream and rose, I bet you have. Damn these commercials and all those royal movies where the princess takes a bath with rose petals in total luxury. For commoners like us its the Soulflower Rose soap. It power packed with the smell of Gulkand to be more precise.

Gulkand is a sweet preserve of rose petals and smells diabetically sweet. Incase you have ever tried the sweet Indian Paan, bang on its the exact same smell.

Price: Rs. 200

Claims: This all natural rose soap is very soothing for the nerves & an anti-depressant. It can calm anger & alleviate hangovers. Rose helps improve the skin's appearance & to perfume skin. Stimulating & cleansing this soap is a good choice for dry, mature, rough or irritated skins.

Contains: Rosehip essential oil, crushed rose petals, Vitamin E, glycerin, aqua, saponified oils of olive, coconut ,palm & palm kernel.

My experience: This is the first soulflower soap which fills up my washroom with aroma, and its lingers for like 8-9 hours, but the aroma is not transfered on skin.

Its a beautiful red colors soap with a very very sweet rose aroma. It lathers well and does not melt like crazy when left in the washroom unattended. It did feel a bit drying to me, but again I have very dry skin.

This one is a party for Rose lovers, to be more specific Gulkand lovers, I find it a bit too much for my nose.

What I like:

  1. Beautiful piece of red soap, which lathers well,
  2. Its like a rose bomb, fills up the washroom for like 8-9 hours,
  3. Does not melt on tray, when kept in washroom,

What I did not like:

  1. I found the rose gulkand smell a bit too sweet for my nose,

Will totally recommend these who like strong aroma and love Rose. People with sensitive nose should stay away.
