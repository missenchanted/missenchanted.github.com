With so many products being launched around this time of the year, its really hard to follow and get everything, specially for a person like me who like to try everything new. I recently received the complete Vivel Cell RenewFortify + Repair Regimen for gorgeous Skin built Cell by Cell range. Its a set of 3 product for complete skin care needs, I so wish had they launched one lip balm too(Although Vivel does have lip balms but not in this range).. and this range would have been more than complete.

The packaging reminds me of Johnson and Johnson range, and I personally like it. Its plain and simple yet sturdy and functional. This range claims to repair skin, cell damage and prevents collagen breakdown. Vivel Cell Renew Fortify + Repair Hand Crème: Price: Rs. 99 for 50ml What I liked:
  1. Moisturizes my hands, without making them greasy,
  2. Moisturizes the cuticle (won't work for super dry or damaged cuticles)
  3. Does not give sweaty palms,
  4. Gets absorbed readily,
  5. I like the smell, it remind me of some old cream from Ponds... or was it Charmis(Not sure...),
  6. Decent price tag for the quantity,
  7. Travel friendly, you can easily carry this one in you handbag

For a person like me who works on computer for 14-15 hours, I like my hand creams to be non greasy yet moisturizing and smell good. So I would definately recommend this to everyone, stay away only if you don't like the fragrance.

I myself will purchase this again for sure. Vivel Cell Renew Fortify + Repair Body Lotion: Price: Rs. 249 for 250ml What I liked:
  1. Pump packaging very hygienic and convenient,
  2. Non greasy
  3. Gets absorbed readily,
  4. Moisturizes well, this will be good for winters as well as summers(Since its not greasy) both

What I did not like:

  1. The smell, I find it a bit medicinal. Though the smell fades away after few minutes of application.
  2. Lacks protection from sun,

All in all its a good moisturizer, and I would recommend this only to those, whom smell does not matter. I personally don't find myself picking this up, as I love fragrant body lotion and cream.

Vivel Cell Renew Fortify + Repair Face Moisturiser Price: Rs. 349 for 50 ml I received this sample with Current month's Femina. What I liked:
  1. Non greasy
  2. Gets absorbed readily,
  3. Even for an oily skin like mine, it gives me a matte finish,
  4. Works as a good makeup base,
What I did not like:
  1. No protection from sun,
  2. Sensitive nose will find the fragrance too stung,
Overall its a good cream for skin. Would recommend this to beauties with dry or flakey skin, you would love it.
