Ok now I am getting hungry..   Price: 299, but Avon comes up with really good offers, Claims:    

My Experience: Although its looks more of deep red, but its its more on the brighter side, and truly lives upto its name COLORLAST, It stayed for like 4-5 hours even after drinking and eating and @ Rs.229 thats a crazy loot. But on second thoughts It can be because of rich pigmentation.

Only drawback is the plastic packaging, which makes it feel cheap, but at this price I ought not complain :P

Initially it looks glossy but dries down to matte, gives complete coverage and does not dry the lips. With staying power of 4-5 hours I so wanna try out some more neutral shades. SURPRISINGLY I cannot locate in in the brochure... please help me out here.. is it discontinued ?

  What I like:
  1. Lasting power, for this particular shade its amazing,
  2. Did not dry out my lips,
  3. Amazing pigmentation,
  4. Absolutely amazing pricing,
What I did not like:
  1. Cannot locate it in the Avon brochure,
  2. Can definitely improve on the packaging,
