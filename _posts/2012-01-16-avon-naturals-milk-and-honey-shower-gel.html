'-' _yoast_wpseo_canonical: '' _yoast_wpseo_redirect: '' --- I have tried quite some stuff from Avon's color and nail range, and this is my first body wash from Avon, which I grabbed on sale again on the onset of winters. Milk and honey was enough to convince me that it will work for me during winters. I have been using this for almost 2 months and have a lot to say about this. Price: Rs. 149 for 200ml, I got this at Rs.129 Claims and Contains: My Experience: This was more of an impulsive buy and I did not have very high expectations, but its has proved itself worth every penny. It comes in a yellow plastic container with a flip cap, which looks decent and rather cute. But I am not very sure that its travel friendly due to its bulky size and flip opening. It smells a blend of vanilla and honey which for me is the biggest plus point. Lathers well, and does not leave me dry. What I like:
  1. Very decently priced,
  2. Love the vanilla and honey smell,
  3. Lathers and cleans well,
  4. Does not leave my skin dry in winters, although a lotion is a must after this.
What I did not like:
  1. Packaging is not travel friendly,
  2. Can be purchased only through Avon consultants.
This is going to last me for some more time, then I would like to try something for Summers. But I might switch back to this for next winters. I would highly recommend this product, at this price it wont even burn a hole into the pocket.
