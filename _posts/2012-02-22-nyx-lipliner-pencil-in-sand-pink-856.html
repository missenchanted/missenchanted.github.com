My experience: I am not a lip liner person, for me just a dab of a nice lipstick with gloss and I am done. So I used this pencil as a matte crayon on my lips and I am really happy with the results. I just wish if these pencils had the capacity to moisturise I would never buy lipsticks again. They don't crease, have a pretty long staying powers and sharpening is not an issue with them.

What I like:

  1. This particular shade is nicely pigmented, and just looks lovely,
  2. Stays on my lips for 4-5 hours, I usually top it with a clear gloss,
  3. 60 shades to choose from, you name it and its there,
  4. Very creamy, and easily glides on the lips, without settling into fine lines,
  5. Sharpening is not an issue here.

What I did not like:

  1. Unlike lipsticks, you need a balm underneath compulsorily, else you might look like a zombie,
  2. A bit steeply priced in India, compared to international prices.

Undoubtedly I would recommend this to everyone, even if you are not a lip lining person. I am going to collect them in more shades for sure.
