Colorbar counter is removed from almost every place I know and one day I hit it at Bangalore Central, Price: Rs.300, its available at discount online Colorbar says: Available in five shades, Plum Brown, Cosmic Rose, Fuchsia, Peachy Rose and Just Earth, the Blusher accentuates the cheek area giving a glow to the face. This talc free, silky powder contains a moisture balance formula that is easy to blend and keeps the skin smooth, hydrated and nourished for soft cheeks with just the right hint of color. This is such a beautiful peachy shade with pink undertones and subtle glitter, What I love about it:
  1. It is such a beautiful shade  :All-I-See-is-Love:
  2. Mirror in it comes in handy so many times,
  3. Very soft and easy to blend,
What I did not like:
  1. Brush is a total turn off, it sheds likes crazy..... literally crazy,
  2. I wish it could last a bit longer, but it fades too soon,
  3. Colorbar has increased the price from Rs.250 to Rs.300.... not liking it :(
Would love to buy this shade again and again. I read on Mehak's blog that she uses this over a cream blush to make it last longer.
