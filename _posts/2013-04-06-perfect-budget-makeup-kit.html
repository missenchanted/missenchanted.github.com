 Budget Makeup Kit under Rs. 1000

I got a lovely mail from Koyal, who was looking for some good makeup products for everyday use but on a tight budget....
Dear Koyal, This post is specially for you, and I have kept my budget limit as Rs.1000. Lets check out what is in the kit,
  1. Maybelline Clearglow BB cream( ~ Rs. 187) - Its the perfect blend for oily to combination skin. Gives decent coverage and glow. I use this on a regular basis.
  2. Lakme Perfect Radiance Compact( ~ Rs. 160) - This has to be the best compact in this price range. Finely milled and blends like dream.
  3. Bio Almond Overnight Therapy Lip Balm( ~ Rs.92) - Its really moisturizing and best for use under lip colors. You can even pick up Maybelline Lip Balms they are also worth every penny.
  4. When I was looking for budget Lip Colors, I had so many choices,
    1. Lakme Enrich Lip Color (~ Rs. 225) - You might go mad browsing through the shades... there are so many to choose from.
    2. Colorbar Velvet Matte Lipstick (~ Rs. 225) - My personal favorite is Bare, its the prefect nude shade.
    3. Maybelline Color Sensational Lipstick(~Rs. 299) - Amazing shade range again.
  5. Maybelline Colossal Kajal(~Rs. 145) - I don't need to praise this one, every college girl has one in her bag.
  6. Maybelline HyperCurl Mascara Black(~Rs. 188) - Best mascara is this budget without any competition.
All the above prices are mostly taken from online shops and add to ~ Rs. 997, so that leaves us with Rs.3 spare for Cadbury ladoo (Kyun mann main laddoo futa). While compiling this list I was amazed that so many products from Maybelline made it to the kit, great quality for amazing price tag.
