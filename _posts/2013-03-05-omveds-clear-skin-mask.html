Omved's Clear Skin Mask

Omved's Clear Skin Mask is a powdered face mask free from Parabens, Sulfates, Silicones, Petrochemicals, Glycols, Phthalates, Synthetic colors and Perfume.
Omved claims:
  • For acne, pimples, enlarged pores, blemishes, pigmentation and sunburns.
  • Remove both surface impurities and deeply embedded toxins and lighten age-spots and pigmentation for that radiant glow
Contains: A classical Ayurvedic recipe, this nutrition house of herbs is a blend of 14 highly-prized therapeutic extracts selected for their deep-cleansing and skin-nourishing properties.
  • Nirgundi unclogs pores and regularises sebum production.
  • Anti-inflammatory and anti-fungal Neem flushes toxins, lightens scars and pigmentation.
  • Sandalwood keeps skin fresh and cool.
  • Manjishtha brings a glow and lustre.
  • Fuller’s Earth absorbs excessive sebum.
  • Turmeric heals and nourishes.
  • Aloe Vera gives a feeling of firmness, fitness and youth.
Price: Rs. 150 for 25gm

Packaging: Comes in an airtight Aluminum foil, with complete list of ingredients and details mentioned on it. As the face mask needs to be stored in a cool place free from humidity, I transferred the content to a tin container and store it in my refrigerator. How to use: Prepare mask by mixing with water/pure Rose water/Aloe Vera gel into a paste. Apply on face and neck. For daily use, leave on for 5 minutes. For weekly deep cleansing treatments, apply about one-quarter inch thick and leave on the skin for 20 minutes. Then remove the paste with a cool water rinse, to which you could add a little lemon juice or rose water. Moisturize.

My Experience: The Face mask powder smells completely herbal with Karpoora(Camphor) smell slightly overpowering the others. I mix rosewater with a teaspoon of powder. I prefer to leave the mixture aside for another 5 minutes so that powder soaks up all the rose water.

I found the pace pack very cooling and soothing to the skin. It cools down the face a little. A thin layer dries up in 5 minutes, whereas a thick coat can take upto 30 minutes. It does not stretch or dry up the skin after wash. The Haldi(turmeric) content does give a slight yellowish/brownish color to the skin. So I prefer using a face wash after this and then tone and moiturize my face.

I have been using this every alternate day for last 2 weeks. I cant see much difference on my skin though in terms of blemishes. But I am happy with Omved's Clear Skin Mask for firstly its completely natural and free of any chemicals, secondly I like the way it soothes and cools my face which does bring down the complete day's stress. I like the herbal smell, its like having the Havan samagari on your face.

What I like:
  1. Completely natural and safe,
  2. Free from harmful chemicals,
  3. Smells herbal and soothes my senses,
  4. Did not break me out,
  5. Does control oil for my skin
What I did not like:
  1. Will not show immediate results, you have to be pateient and dedicated,
  2. I would prefer if this came in a container rather than a pack, as it needs to be stored air tight and away from light.
I would definately recommend Omved clear skin pack, its economical, completely natural and safe. *Product sent by brand for consideration. However, this has not affected my opinion.
