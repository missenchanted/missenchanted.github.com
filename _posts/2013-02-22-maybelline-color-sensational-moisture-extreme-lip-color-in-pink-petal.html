Maybelline Color sensational Moisture Extreme Lip color in Pink Petal Review & Swatches

Pink Petal, a pink color on petal shaped lips. This lipstick seems to be revolving around in many vanity bags. Maybelline Color sensational Moisture Extreme Lip color in Pink Petal is my most reached lipstick off late. A perfect pink is all it takes to define this lipstick shade. Price: Rs.250

This lipstick is indeed moisturizing to my lips. It does not get clumped on the dry areas of my lips too.  Glides evenly on the lips giving a neat glow to the lips. One swipe of the lipstick gives an amazing pink, reminds me of the pink rose. But I am not satisfied with the color coverage for my dark lips, though not very dark. I do have some amount of pigmented lips. I use full 3 swipes heavily and press my lips on a blotting paper to make sure that the lipstick does not bleed due to my heavy application. Since I blot, it looks as though my lips are naturally stained with the shade of pink petal. I top up this stain with my usual lip balm. The lipstick stays on me for more than 5 hours. That is a good amount of time for me.

Swatch: top- single swipe; bottom- 3 swipes.

Packaging is the standard type of twist up tube lipstick . Both tube and cap is a bright poppy pink color. There is no see through portion in the tube to identify the color. Since I have one shade from the range, but for some one with many lipsticks from the collection, it is a tough task to find a shade. On the whole, I like this product, worth the try. Especially, Pink Petal can be a purchase without any hesitation. Pros of Maybelline Color sensational Moisture Extreme Lip color in Pink Petal:
  1. Moisturizing lipstick.
  2. Does not get clumped.
  3. Does not settle into fine lines.
  4. A single swipe gives a good color to the lips.
  5. Staying power is good.
  6. Easy handling twist tube.
  7. Travel friendly package
Cons of Maybelline Color sensational Moisture Extreme Lip color in Pink Petal:
  1. No color indication in  the tube pack is provided.
