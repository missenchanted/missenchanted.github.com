Lacto Calamine Classic

This is a life saver, I have been using Lacto Calamine Classic for last 10 years :). I don't remember who introduced me to this great product, but I do remember using it during summers back in my home town. Initially my hostel mates thought it was kinda foundation because of its pink color :D he he

Price: Rs. 95 for 120ml Product boasts of: Discover Skinsurance with Lacto calamine - The goodness of Clay & Glycerin gives your skin:
  1. Nourishment,
  2. Acne and Oil control
  3. Dead skin removal
  4. Protection.
Apply daily for clear, beautiful skin Product contains: Aqua, Light Kaolin, Castor Oil, Glycerin, Zinc Oxide, Zinc Carbonate, Emulsifiers, Preservatives, Perfumes and Permited colours Instruction: Shake well before use and for external purpose only.

What I Love about the product:
  1. All the contents are safe and beneficial for the skin health.
  2. Consistency is correct and gives coverage to skin.
  3. Gets absorbed into skin and gives a shine free look.
  4. Pricing is so correct :)
  5. Handy packing and can be carried in hand bag
  6. It does remove dead skin slowly
  7. It gives that soothing effect to the complete face.
What I dont like :
  1. No Sun, protection, so need to put on a sheer SPF powder OR cream on top :(
