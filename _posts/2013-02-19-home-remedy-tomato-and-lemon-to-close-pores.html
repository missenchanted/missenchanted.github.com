Kitchen picks: Tomato face masks

Tales of miraculous tomato from the refrigerator accompanied with Lemon. Tomato face masks  are most effective way to close pores, naturally exfoliate and lighten skin tone (remove tan)

Face Mask for Oily skin : Take one tablespoon of fresh tomato juice(just squeeze out a tomato), mix it with 4-5 drop of lime juice. Apply this juice mixture all over your face and let it dry for 15 minutes. Rinse it off with lukewarm water and then apply your regular toner I sometimes add 1-2 drops of Tea tree oil, to counter act against my acne issues.
Tomato juice has been found to be effective in temporary closing the pores. So if you make it a regular routine, you will never need any other cream to fight against open pores.
Face Mask for Dry skin : Take one tablespoon of fresh tomato juice and mix it with little honey. Apply this mixture over your face with a cotton and let it dry for 15 minutes. You will see the results yourself in smooth and glowing skin Home made remedies can never go wrong. They are mild, natural and effective.
