Indian online shopping arena, is growing exponentially, so I compiled a list of major players in the game, hope this helps you guys and please do not forget to share your ratings and comments.

Indian Websites

1. Fashion and You

Fashion and You offers some amazing discounts, which are hard to skip. But they ship almost all order after 3-4 weeks of order placement. They did have some glitches in initial stages but I am a fully satisfied customer now. I have shopped 7 times with them.

Review

2. Brandmile

Brandmile has some great offers sometimes, I have shopped 5 times with them. Although I did not have any issue with them, but I feel they lack in variety and product range. They also take 3 weeks to ship. I have got words from few friends of mine about damaged or under quality products. Review

3. 99labels

99labels is again a great place to buy international brands at great discounts, I have shopped 5 times with them. They too take 3 weeks for shipping. I don't find there sales tempting though.

4. Stylecraze

This has to be one of my current favorite website. Customer service is very helpful and very prompt, no doubt best in the market. I have shopped 8 times with them. Review

5. Violetbag

I have shopped only once with them. They are a great website too, and offer discounts but somehow I always end up buying from others. Review

6. Urbantouch

According to my knowledge they were amongst the first entrants in the Online cosmetics market in India. Nowdays discounts have almost dried up but they still stock a very large list of brands. What I love the most is their shipping box, I keep them adding to my collection of boxes. Review

7. Flipkart

This has to be my favorite shopping website. Their delivery and customer service is just excellent. They give amazing discounts on books and electronics, that I never go checking around for stuff anymore. Till date I have bought my Laptop, Galaxy tab, 4-5 mobiles and hundreds of books from here.

8. Ebay

I am still pretty confused, since myself I have never struck a star deal here. But some of my amazing friend swear by it. Review

9. Lush India

Its a boon for people who don't have access to LUSH stores, but I am going to skip it due to shipping charges. But I still look out for some offers some times. Review

10. Healthkart

This site has a vast collection ranging from health to makeup and cosmetics. And it sure has some amazing deals. Review

11. Retailmart

I have shopped with them once, but truly speaking nothing amazing. I had gone there for Khadi which they do not stock any more. Review

12. Natural Mantra

GoGreen, great place for Organic stuff. I look forward for handmade soaps, Jams and pickles. Review

Shopped with them once, they come out with some good weekly offers but for the rest not many amazing discounts. But they do stock some great skin care products. Check out.
